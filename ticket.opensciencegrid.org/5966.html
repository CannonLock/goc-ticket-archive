03 Nov 2008 14:29:20,787 org.glite.ce.monitor.holder.NotificationHolder - Sending notifica

The next to last line is the GOC's aggregator. Is there an error? There should be since we aren't
receiving the data. My advice would be to tail this log and watch what happens. It attempts to send
data every ten minutes. Since we've had so many issues with firewalls and packets going into the
bit bucket, I suspect port 14001 outgoing from your CE isn't open or there's something networking-
wise at work. You can use the netstat command to see what is happening on port 14001.
