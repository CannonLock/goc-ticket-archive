    Jul 10, 2014 03:50 PM UTC by Igor Sfiligoi 
    Hi Richard.

You must define this both in the match_exprt and the start_expr.
(the later is not defined in the default XML, but defaults to True, just like the match_expr)

Here is what CMS uses (simplified):
match_expr='glidein["attrs"]["GLIDEIN_Site"] in job["DESIRED_Sites"].split(",")'
start_expr='stringListMember(GLIDEIN_Site,DESIRED_Sites,",")=?=True'

Not exactly your use case, but should give you the idea.

Hope this helps,
Igor
