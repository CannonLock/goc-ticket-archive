Hi Doug,

Recently we had some problems because the NorduGrid CA cert had expired,
and a lot of Atlas users transfer files between BNL and NorduGrid sites
every day.  Apparently this cert was updated by the IGTF last week, but
this update was never included in a VDT/OSG update, nor were we warned
about its expiration.

What is the OSG policy for watching and updating the CA certs?  Is there
currently a CA cert monitoring system in place?   Shouldn't the VDT
and/or OSG be monitoring the CA certs, updating the CA package before
they expire and send warnings to all of the sites and/or VO support
centers?  It shouldn't be left up to each site to monitor the ones that
they need, right?
