    Thanks for this information. A local user seems to have a badly behaving job
which is filling /tmp on some of our worker nodes. Normally Condor will not
start new jobs if the /tmp (OSG_WNTMP) space is below a certain threshold. What
seems to be happening is that this users local Condor job is starting when
there is plenty of free space and running until /tmp is full. We are contacting
the user and working on cleaning up the affected nodes.
