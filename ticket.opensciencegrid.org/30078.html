In February 2015 it was proposed that all LHC Tier-1s will be advertising via BGP towards LHCOPN an IPv6 peering, including an onsite dual-stack Perfsonar server.

The following roadmap was agreed upon: beginning of April 2015

Up to today those Tier-1 sites have it already deployed:

CERN, CNAF, FNAL, IN2P3, NGDF, NL-T1, KIT
