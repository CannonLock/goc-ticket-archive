Your vomses and voms2.fnal lsc file are out of date. Please note that ONLY voms2 has changed. Just updating your VO package should fix it, but if you want to do it manually:

/DC=org/DC=opensciencegrid/O=Open Science Grid/OU=Services/CN=voms2.fnal.gov
/DC=org/DC=cilogon/C=US/O=CILogon/CN=CILogon OSG CA 1
