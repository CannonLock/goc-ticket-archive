    Hi,

there is a longstanding issue in transfers from Purdue to PIC and CNAF which is likely related to jumble frame support on GEANT. It would be nice if some network admins may help the sites to debug this problem.

Here is some data:
- the problem appeared in Jan 2010 between Purdue and PIC and in Jul 2010 between Purdue and CNAF.

https://cmsweb.cern.ch/phedex/debug/Activity:;:QualityPlots?graph=quality_all&entity=link&src_filter=Purdue&dest_filter=CNAF|PIC&no_mss=true&period=l52w&upto=

- Here is the link to the CMS Savannah ticket about this problem:

https://savannah.cern.ch/support/index.php?116101

- A similar problem showed for PIC/CNAF -> Purdue transfers (same starting date) and was solved on Aug 2010. Here is the link to the related CMS Savannah ticket

https://savannah.cern.ch/support/?116025
