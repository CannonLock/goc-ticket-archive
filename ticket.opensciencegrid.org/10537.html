    Jun 13, 2011 03:58 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    We are no longer seeing jobs fail with this problem. The issue was described by
Sarah above, but to summarize:

There were two files causing these jobs failures. The first was deleted by
central deletion services before jobs which required it had a chance to access
them.

The second was erroneously being reported as missing to our xrootd clients by
the our xrootd server. This stopped happening after an hour or so, and the jobs
have succeeded ever since. We have investigated this problem and determined
that it was a caching issue on our xrootd redirector, and have hopefully
addressed it with an updated to our xrdlfc plugin.

So, this issue seems to be cleared up. We are not failing jobs due to lsm-get
currently, so i believe this ticket can be considered closed. Please re-open if
any new issues arise, or start a new ticket.

Cheers,
