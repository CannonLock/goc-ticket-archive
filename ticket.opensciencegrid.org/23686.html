    Jan 29, 2015 04:12 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi all,
premature EOF errors usually occur for one of two reasons:

1. a network device decides to cut the connection for some reason;
2. the security configuration is wrong on one of the endpoints.

Please check if the firewall configs of your SRM and all its disk servers
are consistent and compatible with exemptions in department, campus
or further upstream equipment (are the hosts e.g. white-listed, if needed).

And/or check if the security configs (CAs, CRLs, VOMS LSC files,
grid-mapfiles, ...) are the same on the SRM and all its disk servers.

If locally all looks good, there may be a network issue upstream,
which may be a lot harder to debug...  :-(
