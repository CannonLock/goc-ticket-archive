Hello Purdue admins,

Since about 18:00 PDT yesterday we have seen the factory steadily lose track of glideins at Purdue.  By losing track I mean that from the factory point of view they are reported as running, but in reality at the site they are not running.

I ran a fork job to examine the queue at osg.rcac.purdue.edu and it appears the majority of these glideins were running but are now idle.

The majority of these glideins are submitted from the gluex VO, and a good amount were submitted from glow VO as well.

Can you please let us know what may have caused the pilots to go from running back to idle state?  Could it have been due to some kind of preemption or priority policies?  From the factory point of view these glideins are lost forever, even if they do start back up again and successfully run.

If you have any details about what could be causing this please let us know.

Thanks,
