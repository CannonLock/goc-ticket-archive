    Hi Soichi,

Here is the use case -- every month I present a set of metrics to the US T2 sites, which has for a while now included the day-by-day status of the RSV tests.  This lets me embarrass sites that are doing poorly, in the hope that this will inspire them to do better.  Having the history day by day allows sites to go back and check specific problems they might have had at that time.  So it is very nice to have a compact way to display this information that can be shown in a single slide, or as part of a single slide.  I suppose the history could be considered less crucial than just the average over a given period...but it still seems useful.

So, of the list of differences you give, I suppose #3 is the most critical one.  I suppose a version like what you linked in #1 would be useful, but I'd need it to be much more compact, and I would be happy if it just summarizes all the RSV tests for a given site, both CE (multiple CE's, even) and SE.  As for #2, could we define a resource group that is the US CMS T2 sites?  (Plus the Brazil CMS T2 sites, which we always bring along with us to be friendly.)  That would make it easier to get the sites I want, especially as I can never remember their OSG names, as they follow no uniform convention.

Also, I am on that mailing list...I enjoy getting the emails each morning.  Thanks, best wishes.
