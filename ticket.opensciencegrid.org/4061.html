Hi Craig,
I just digged an email saying
"you have to modify
$OSG_LOCATION/lcg/etc/add-attributes.conf and/or
$OSG_LOCATION/lcg/etc/alter-attributes.conf.
Manual modification of $OSG_LOCATION/lcg/var/gip/ldif/*.ldif is
overridden" like you said.
So, in  this case, I think you need to modify alter-attributes.conf.

I think this is the way, but we need to experiment this unless someone
shows up to help us:

Take these three ldif's that have jobmanager-pbs-batchq:
/grid/osg/osg-ce-0.6/lcg/var/gip/{osg-info-static-ce.ldif,osg-info-stat
ic-cesebind.ldif,osg-info-static-cluster.ldif}

and replace jobmanager-pbs-batchq with jobmanager-pbs-submit
(fortunately nothing's chopped off and you can use sed command)

and cat them into $OSG_LOCATION/lcg/etc/alter-attributes.conf

I guess that's it.
There is an option to reconfigure the GIP, but I don't think you want
to think about it?
Bockjoo
