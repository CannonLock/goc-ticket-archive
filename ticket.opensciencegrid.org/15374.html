Hi Von,

No problem. If you have your new Digicert cert in your browser
and you remove your old DOEGrids cert. I think it will do the
right thing for you when you apply for OSG DocDB access.

If it doesn't work the first time, you can try quitting and
restarting your browser and trying after that.

If you are not sure how to remove your DOEGrids cert,
Desktop support or I can give you help with that.

-- Laura

2013-06-05 12:00:47 CDT - Laura Mengel (Additional comments)
Hi Von, It sounds like you have the old certificate in your browser
and this is what is being sent when you look at OSG DocDB, so
that is why you are seeing the group list for your old certificate.
After noting the groups you are in for the old certificate, can you try:
- removing your doegrids cert from your browser
- adding the new digicert cert in your browser if it's not there already
- going to that same OSG DocDB certificate application form
https://osg-docdb.opensciencegrid.org:440/cgi-bin/CertificateApplyForm
Now it should behave like you are making a fresh application for your
new cert (which you are) and you can pick the groups needed etc. and
submit..
Please give this a try and let us know if it works for you.
-- Laura Mengel, DocDB Admin Staff
