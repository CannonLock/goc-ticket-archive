The VDT announcement is a general announcement for everything in the
VDT, and it's not specific to a subset. If you don't have the
Gratia-Reporting package, then don't update it.

Most CE installations in OSG don't have Gratia-Reporting. It's the
"Gratia Collector", which lets you collect statistics locally. Not many
people are using it yet, but they could use it, and it might even be
installed on their CE.
---------------------------------------------------------------
