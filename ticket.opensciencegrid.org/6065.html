    Dec 3, 2008 04:27 PM UTC by Parag Mhashilkar 
    Hi Arvind,

You can increase the log level by changing parameters for log4j in
tomcat/v55/webapps/ce-monitor/WEB-INF/classes/log4j.properties add
"error" and/or info and/or warn as below.

You may also want to increase the size of
log4j.appender.fileout.MaxFileSize  to 20MB+ to prevent the logs being
rotated fast.

-------
log4j.logger.org.glite.security=error, fileout, info, warn

log4j.logger.org.glite=info, fileout, error, warn
log4j.appender.fileout=org.apache.log4j.RollingFileAppender
log4j.appender.fileout.File=/data/ress/vdt-1.10.1-m/glite/var/log/glite-ce-monitor.log
log4j.appender.fileout.MaxFileSize=20MB
log4j.appender.fileout.MaxBackupIndex=20
log4j.appender.fileout.layout=org.apache.log4j.PatternLayout
log4j.appender.fileout.layout.ConversionPattern=%d{dd MMM yyyy
HH:mm:ss,SSS} %c- %m%n
