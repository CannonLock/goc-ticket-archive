    Jul 20, 2017 11:55 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi Geonmo,

It seems I was following a wrong path, but one that, nevertheless, luckily I hit this soon.
Details are here: https://its.cern.ch/jira/browse/DMC-974

TL;DR the version of globus-gssapi-gsi on epel-testing enables empty fragments. This doesn't seem to work with Bestman, who probably wrongly interprets this fragment as a '0', which is followed  by the beginning of the actual payload (POST ....)

That's why I saw this only on the development machines.

So, we will have to ask Mattias to hold on this update, or patch it, until it is decided how to proceed.
