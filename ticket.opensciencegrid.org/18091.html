The "sent successfully" count indicates if you are actually sending data.
The "suppressed" count indicates a problem with your
/var/lib/osg/user-vo-map file.  These are likely grid-proxy jobs that need that file.

However, the fact that the log isn't showing much activity, there is no data in /var/lib/gratia/data
to send up.  Usually, the PER_JOB_HISTORY_DIR issue.

Let me know what you find.
