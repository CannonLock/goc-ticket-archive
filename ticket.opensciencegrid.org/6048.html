    Dec 3, 2008 08:27 PM UTC by GLOW-TECH 
    I believe the problem is that there are a number of really huge condor
job logs on
osggate.clemson.edu:$OSG_LOCATION/globus/tmp/gram_job_state.  For example:

-rw-r--r--  1 glow   glow    8099229 Nov 25 22:06
gram_condor_log.12547.1226970338

The grid_manager_monitor_agent script reads through all of the job logs
belonging to a user and I suspect that these huge jobs logs are causing
the script to eat up a lot of cpu time.

The reason the logs are so huge is that they contain a huge number of
JobDisconnectedEvent and JobReconnectFailedEvent records.  I believe
this was caused by frequent restarts of the schedd on Nov 25.  The
restarts were caused by the schedd running out of file descriptors.  I
do not know why that happened.

I recommend removing all of the glow jobs in the queue again and
verifying that this also cleans up the large condor_log files in
globus/tmp/gram_job_state.

If the schedd starts crashing and restarting again, please save the
condor daemon logs.  (You should get obituary notices when condor
daemons die.  These are currently being sent to condor-admin@.....)

--Dan
