    Jun 26, 2012 09:18 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hello,

the right way to remove a mysql VO, as stated in the documentation, is:

voms-admin-configure remove --vo UC3 --undeploy-database --dropdb --dbapwd ***

If you use the above command no trace should be left of the former VO (at the database level).

The presence of the

/etc/tomcat5/Catalina/localhost/voms#UC3.xml

file is due to the fact that, before removing the VO, you should take care of undeploying the VO from tomcat,
using
service voms-admin stop UC3

Let me know if the above hints solve your issues.

Cheers,
Andrea
