    Nov 11, 2008 09:36 PM UTC by Tim Silvers 
    Chris,

Several weeks ago we worked with the Fermilab Support Center to be sure that Gratia-related
tickets were included in our ticket exchange. In other words, if we open a ticket for Gratia, it goes
to Remedy, the Fermilab ticketing system, and by keying off of fields in our ticket, routes the
ticket to the Gratia queue in Remedy. This provides tracking and other benefits that I believe
Fermilab wanted.

As for the tickets themselves, we opened tickets for a few Resources back in October (view the
ticket at https://oim.grid.iu.edu/gocticket/viewer?id=5854). All but one of them (the Florida
resource) is closed. The tickets were assigned to the GRATIA agent in our system and tickets should
have been opened in Remedy, however, looking at some of them now, I might have an idea why
they weren't. I understand that the Destination VO Support Center field on our end needs to be set
to GRATIA or Remedy won't route the ticket to the GRATIA Remedy queue. We'll look into that.

If you have questions about this, let us know.

Tim
