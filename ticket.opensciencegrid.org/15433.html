    Sep 27, 2013 04:24 PM UTC by Neha Sharma 
    Looking at your gums configuration file, you do not have 'bestmanAccountMapping' included in the 'groupToAccountMappings' for host 'smuosg1.hpc.smu.edu'.
Currently it looks like -
<hostToGroupMapping
groupToAccountMappings='newAtlas, newUsatlas, newUsatlasProd, newUsatlasSoft, mis, rsvAccountMapping, nova, nova-production, novaana, novagli'
description=''
cn='smuosg1.hpc.smu.edu'/>

The first groupToAccountMapping  in the list is 'newAtlas' which is configured to use usergroup 'newatlas'  and map the users to local account 'usatlas4'.
The FQAN in question - /atlas/Role=production/Capability=NULL satisfies the mapping conditions for this usergroup and hence gets mapped to this user.

You need to add the user/robot DN to the manual user group named 'bestman' and then change above 'hostToGroupMapping' to look like

<hostToGroupMapping
groupToAccountMappings='bestmanAccountMapping, newAtlas, newUsatlas, newUsatlasProd, newUsatlasSoft, mis, rsvAccountMapping, nova, nova-production, novaana, novagli'
description=''
cn='smuosg1.hpc.smu.edu'/>
