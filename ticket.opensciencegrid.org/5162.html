    Hi Terrance,

> If I am publishing here why would I not publish wherever ReSS is?

Because it goes through a different path. CEMon sends information out to
two Information Gatherers, one is the RAW LDIF information that goes to
the BDII and the other is in the CLASSAD format which goes to the ReSS.

The validation page for ReSS information can be found at
https://osg-ress-1.fnal.gov:8443/ReSS/ReSS-prd-History.html
You can also check through the following command line
% condor_status -pool osg-ress-1.fnal.gov -constraints 'GlueSiteName ==
"UCSDT2-B"' -l

To being with check glite/var/log/glite-ce-monitor.log and
tomcat/v55/logs/catalina.out, to see if any errors are being recorded. I
suspect there would be something there indicating that the transmission
to osg-ress-1 is failing.

If you need further help you could send us these files.

Thanks
Anand
