    Since there was a problem with the Footprints <-> Remedy ticket exchange, I'm sending this ticket
to USCMS for the first time.

Tim

Detailed description:
Some sites are publishing their SRM v2 endpoints with "GlueServiceType: srm_v2". glite-sd queries
(used to construct services.xml at FTS serves) are not able to parse correctly the GlueServiceType:
srm_v2 published endpoints.

There is a hack in glite-sd-query to make "srm_v1" be accepted as type
"SRM". The hack does not exist for "srm_v2".

The proper way would be sites publishing GlueServiceType: SRM and the version 1 and/or 2 in the
GlueServiceVersion tag.

At the moment, 5 SRMv2 endpoints are not visible by FTS servers, hence cannot attempt any
transfer with their SRMv2 endpoints. Additionally, 214 sites might need a revision of the srm_v1 tag
in GlueServiceType.

[cms001@vobox06 cms001]$ ldapsearch -h bdii.pic.es -p 2170 -x -b mds-vo-name=local,o=grid
"(&(objectClass=GlueService)(|(GlueServiceType=SRM) (GlueServiceType=srm*)))" | grep
"GlueServiceType" | grep srm_v2 | wc -l
