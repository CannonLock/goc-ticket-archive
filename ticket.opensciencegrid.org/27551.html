    This ticket was opened on behalf of Ale Di Girolamo, ATLAS Distributed Computing Operations co-coordinator:

Dear ATLAS Site Responsible,
to minimize the operational efforts on your side for what concern storage consistency checks, we have developed a tool to centrally take care of the storage consistency checks.
The (New) rucio-auditor daemon can perform consistency checks for all ATLAS sites. For it to work we need that the sites publish one dump per disk endpoint using the schema described in:
https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/AtlasComputing/DDMDarkDataAndLostFiles#Automated_checks_Site_Responsibi

If you have any question, please do not hesitate to contact the ATLAS Distributed Data Management team: atlas-adc-ddm-support@....
