    Hi,

Please see:
https://www.racf.bnl.gov/docs/howto/grid/gridcertfaq#section-6

In short: once the initial download session has expired, the P12 version of the certificate is no loner available. You'll need to renew that certificate (if possible), or have the certificate invalidated and request a new one.
