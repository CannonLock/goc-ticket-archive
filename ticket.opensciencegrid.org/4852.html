I see you already have a ticket open on this issue, so I closed the new ticket you opened and
pasted your description from that ticket into this one (see below). In response to the issue, just to

DN belongs to the MIS VO, a VO different from the VO your DN is a member. You're most likely a
member of SBGrid.

- no existence of local user account for local VO mapping (did you create a local mis user?)

/DC=org/DC=doegrids/OU=People/CN=Tim Silvers (GridScan) 387103

your system logs for errors or clues.
