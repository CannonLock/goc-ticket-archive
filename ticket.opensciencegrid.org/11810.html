    Mar 20, 2012 09:27 PM UTC by Igor Sfiligoi 
    The problem is the delegation Condor does from the CE to the WN;
since the proxy is limited once it lands on the CE (by Globus), doing another delegation will result in an invalid proxy.
(BTW: Condor should be smarter about this!)

The solution to this is to disable delegation in Condor, and have the proxy copied over to the WN (by Condor).
A little less secure, but I guess you trust you local LAN.

To do this, as Dave said, put
DELEGATE_JOB_GSI_CREDENTIALS = False
in you CE condor config
(i.e. /etc/condor/condor_config*)

Cheers,
Igor

PS: Could not find this documented on the OSG CE installation page!
