    A stratum one snapshot occurs every 15 minutes at the GOC, once per hour at the other US replica. Propagation to the worker nodes
is not under central control and varies from site to site. I would suggest a test, push an update and see what you get. The system is
not designed for rapidly changing content, I  usually allow a day for content to propagate everywhere.
