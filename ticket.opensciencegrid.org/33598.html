FNAL is requesting a lot of data from T1_FR_CCIN2P3, and due to a network bottleneck between the dCache pools and the export pools, an important backlog got created [1].

Would it be possible to reduce the transfers requests to CCIN2P3 so the load decreases and we would be able to recover  the system.
