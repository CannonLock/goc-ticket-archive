    John,

We run four gatekeepers at MWT2 (resources MWT2, MWT2_UC, MWT2_IU and MWT2_UIUC)

All four inject jobs into the MWT2 Condor pools at located at UChicago, Indiana and Illinois
Due to Condor flocking, the MWT2 site is available even if only one of our four gatekeepers is online.
The daily availability reports are misleading for our site as a result.

The resources MWT2 and MWT2_UC  (gatekeepers) are located at UChicago.

For most of last year, MWT2 handled EL5 nodes at UChicago (all other gatekeepers were for EL6 nodes)
Since Atlas could not run on EL5 nodes, only Opportunistic VOs (osg, glow, engage, etc) jobs were run
via this gatekeeper on the EL5 nodes. Thus no Atlas gratia records would be seen for the MWT2 resource
during this time frame.

We have now retired the last of the remaining EL5 nodes at Midwest Tier 2. and reource MWT2
will now be part of a EL6 pool. As a result Atlas jobs will soon be run via this resource..

As yet, we have not resumed Atlas jobs from APF via this gatekeeper but will soon.
At which point you should see gratia records for Atlas from MWT2.

I hope this clarifies the MWT2 resource?

dave
