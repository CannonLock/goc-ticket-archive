    I'm the site administrator for the LCG site we are attempting to get
running at BNL. Three previous BNL employees have attempted to get a
site up and running, with no success. I've *almost* got it going, but
still have a lot of problems.

So I've been submitting tickets to the GGUS to try to get help setting
up the site. *Site* support for BNL-LCG2 is supposed to be handled by
the CERN_ROC (Regional Operations Center--since there really isn't an
LCG support center for North America). What happens is that the GGUS
folks are used to routing *user* site problems to the ROC for the site,
and for *user* problems, they really are supposed to route them through
the OSG GOC (and you'll forward them on to me to handle them).

I think I've gotten them to recognize that *site administrator* tickets
need to go to the CERN_ROC. Sorry for the confusion. In the future you
can assume that any tickets from me that get forwarded to you by GGUS
are a mistake. Tickets from a user, though, should properly be directed
to me.

Hope this makes sense,

Cheers,

--john
