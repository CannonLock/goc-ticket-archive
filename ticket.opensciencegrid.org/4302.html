osg.local:2119/jobmanager-condor

Probably, when you ran configure_osg or configure_gip, the scripts
picked up the local interface, instead of the public interface.

You might want to try the configuration again. You can see locally if
you have solved the problem by sourcing "setup.sh" in your VDT
installation, then
> $VDT_LOCATION/glite/etc/glite-ce-ce-plugin/glite-ce-info > /tmp/out
> grep GlueCEInfoContactString /tmp/out
Typically, interface binding for the system is defined in /etc/hosts.

At this time, FSU-HEP does not show up on the information system. This
may be indicative of other problems: for example, is your tomcat running?

To see if your site advertises to the information system, you can execute
> condor_status -pool osg-ress-1.fnal.gov -const
"GlueSiteName==\"FSU-HEP\""
You should get several output lines.
