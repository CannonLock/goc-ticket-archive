    Dear admin,

Your site-local-config.xml file is currently not listing the backup squids for Frontier, which help prevent jobs to fail due to an overloading of your site squid(s) and/or the CERN Launchpads (central Frontier servers). Since you are in the Americas region, you need to include the following lines in the <frontier-connect> section of the xml:

<backupproxy url=http://cmsbproxy.fnal.gov:3128/>;
<backupproxy url=http://cmsbpfrontier.cern.ch:3128/>;

Best regards,

Luis
CMS Computing Operations
