Since the total quota for the whole gluex VO in this area on FermiGrid is
only 10GB, you will run out of quota after only about 15 jobs.
Plus it's horribly inefficient io-wise to have worker nodes write to NFS
like that.  You should

cd $OSG_WN_TMP
and then make a subdirectory underneath that,
before you untar/transfer your files.

I also note that these jobs are coming with a pilot job cert but
your jobs are not using gLexec.  That's in violation of Fermilab policy,
you must use gLexec if you are going to run here.
