    NOTE to OSG folks, ignore this particular comment, it's intended for Hideki only and isn't relevant to the belle.cern.ch in oasis issue.

Curious as to why #3 wasn't satisfied as well, but I have a possible theory. We run all jobs at T2_US_Nebraska in a chroot environment, and for the most part either bind mount or symlink various filesystems into the chroot so jobs see them. In the case of CVMFS, we do all the actual mounts in the chroot path, but have symlinks into that area for the non-chroot view (eg: regular OS things, admin interaction, etc). Out of the three CVMFS repos we mount, belle.cern.ch was the only one we don't have a symlink from the non-chroot /cvmfs into the chrooted /cvmfs. My understanding is that jobs executing are placed in the chroot immediately, and thus it shouldn't matter at all that this symlink was missing, but -- perhaps it does. Hard to ignore a case where one out of three otherwise identical filesystems isn't found.

I've added this symlink so the temporary belle.cern.ch CVMFS mount looks exactly like our other two (which do work fine) - should be across all workers within the next 10-15 minutes. Feel free to shoot some more jobs this way and let us know if it does/doesn't work. Once the oasis repo is corrected I'll remove all of this of course.
