    Hi again,

just one quick additional comment:

> It looks like specific nodes are affected: compute-4-10.private.dns.zone
> to compute-4-20.private.dns.zone and compute-4-3.private.dns.zone to
> compute-4-9.private.dns.zone

I don't think there's a problem with these nodes in particular,
I think this is just an artifact of our Condor configuration.
These are our newest and fastest nodes, therefore Condor will schedule
new jobs preferably there when they are idle, since we've configured
Condor that way, to optimize throughput. So once the site is drained
because of the site being turned off, all new jobs will start
on these nodes.

I still think the real problem are the corrupted releases 16.6.7 and 16.6.8,
and hopefully once they're re-installed, everything will be better.

Alessandro or Lorenzo, please let us know when you think we should
turn the queue back to test to make sure everything it back to normal.

Thanks a lot,

Horst
