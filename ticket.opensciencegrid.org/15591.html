    Hi Jorge,

The keys are located inside the RPMs themselves. Most users do not need
the keys installed ahead of time, since they are installing the RPMs
directly with "rpm" rather than via "yum". All repository keys are
located in /etc/pki/rpm-gpg, so if you need them ahead of time, you can
install the RPMs manually and copy the files out of there. (The key
files are RPM-GPG-KEY-OSG and RPM-GPG-KEY-EPEL-5 or -6).

As for osg-el5-release-latest.rpm vs. osg-release, it's a symlink so
users wouldn't have to know the exact name and version of the RPM and
to simplify the URL that they have to type to do the install.

-Mat
