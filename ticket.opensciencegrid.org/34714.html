GOCTX Source: https://ggus.eu/ws/ticket_info.php?ticket=130115
    Aug 20, 2017 08:07 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi,

In the last two days, BNL's dark data kept decreasing. As it showed in plot, we
are clear of dark data today.
We also find the reason for dark data. Large volume of data (about
1212Terabyte) were deleted from dCache namespace since 08/12. The cleaner
worked in full speed and removed all of these deleted files from dCache pools
on time. But SRMSpaceManager has latency (seems like two days) to update its
database. For example, files deleted and cleaned on 08/19 still had records in
SRM database. The latency is reflected in BNL's SRM space usage report. That is
the reason when large number of deletion going on, dark data occurs.

We will contact SRM developers for this issue, at the mean time ensure updated
information in the SRM database .

Iris
