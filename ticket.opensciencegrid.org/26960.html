    Dear site admins,

As you have already heard, we need to update all DBParam passwords for all
tiers. This operation needs to be synchronized among all sites and PhEDEx, so
we have picked the following date for the change:

Tuesday, November 3rd, 2015

We need your full collaboration in this, or your site will end up without
access to PhEDEx. Before the deadline above, your site has to nominate one, and
only one (in case you have several) administrator who will receive the new
DBParam file and install it. The new file will be encrypted by the public key
of that admin. So, there are four things you, at your site, need to do/check:

0) Acknowledge the reception of this message, and confirm that your site will
take the rest of the actions bellow before the deadline mentioned above.

1) Pick an admin who will be available on the date agreed above to do the job.

2) Check that the selected admin is registered in
[https://cmsweb.cern.ch/sitedb/prod/sites SiteDB] as either a "PhEDEx Contact"
or "Site Admin" for your site. We will not trust certificates of people not
registered in SiteDB as admins at the site.

3) Attach the certificate (usercert.pem) of the selected admin to this ticket.
(Please pay attention to attach the public-readable certificate 'usercert.pem'
and NOT the private key 'userkey.pem', because this will make the private key
visible to the world, and will force us to revoke the certificate altogether.)

In case you need to change the selected admin in the last moment, we will
accept the latest certificate attached to this ticket, provided it meets all
the requirements above.

We thank you in advance for your cooperation in this collaboration-wide effort,
and hope that it will run smoothly for your site!

    
With Best Regards,
PhEDEx and Transfers teams.
