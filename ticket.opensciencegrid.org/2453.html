    Greetings,

Is this maintenance complete?

Downtime Thursday Sep 7, 9 AM to noon

We will be upgrading the USCMS/FNAL dCache resilient pools to the next
release of
v1.7 jars. DCache access to the resilient pools will be offline from 9
to noon.
DCache access to the production pools will remain online and available.
Most people
use the production pools and should not notice this outage.

We will also be upgrading the dccp and SRM clients to the latest
versions on all
USCMS/FNAL facility nodes. This should be a transparent change for
users.

PLANNED NEXT WEEK: Our plan is to upgrade ssh on the USCMS UAF to the
latest version.
This means users with "modern versions" of client ssh applications will
be able to
connect to the UAF load balancer again and not have to use the direct
access nodes.
Users on lxplus at CERN will have to use the FNAL ssh client until CERN
updates their
cluster in October.

Respectfully,
Camille
