    Fu-Ming,

Are you saying the trusted certificate on your end might be out of date? You can check that by
issuing the following command like I did:

openssl x509 -in ../../osg-0.7.1/globus/share/certificates-35-1/a692434d.0 -enddate

The result on my ITB node is: notAfter=Jun 16 08:31:54 2008 GMT. You should be updating your
CA package regularly. The 0.8.0 package includes the updater to automatically do this for you. If
you're not running that package, you can update with:

pacman -update CA-Certificates
