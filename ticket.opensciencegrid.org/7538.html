    Sep 30, 2009 12:35 AM UTC by wart@.... 
    The problem was a stale condor_master process that was never killed
after running 'service condor-cron stop' (ticket #7540)  For some
reason, even after DNS caching had been cleared, a restart of
condor-cron started up a condor_master process that had retained the
original IP address.

I shut down condor-cron and issued a 'kill -9' on the stale
condor_master process.  After restaring condor-cron, it appears to be
working correctly even after two hours have passed.
