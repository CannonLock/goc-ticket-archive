    Aug 19, 2011 06:12 PM UTC by rynge@.... 
    Hong,

Are you using Firefox or some other browser?

Make sure that your CA certificates has been updated:

Updating the DOEGrids CA Chain

The first step is to ensure you have the latest DOEGrids CA Chain in
your browser.

Browse to https://pki1.doegrids.org/

In the top navigation bar, click Retrieval. - In the left navigation
bar, click Import CA Certificate Chain.

Import the certificate chain. Under Users, ensure that the default
choice, Import the CA certificate chain into your browser, is selected,
and then click Submit.

In the Downloading Certificate dialog, enable all three options to
allow "ESnet Root CA 1" to identify web sites, email users, and software
developers, and then click OK.
