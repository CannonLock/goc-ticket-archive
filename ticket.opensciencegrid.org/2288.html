    Sep 28, 2006 05:26 PM UTC by Steven Timm 
    This ticket can be closed now.  If we see further problems we
will re-open.  You should not necessarily conclude, however,
that the upgrade was the reason that we got rid of the hanging processes.
The hanging processes issue was actually solved before this time
due to detective work by us, monitoring apache sessions and finally
tracing it to an off-site gums server that was polling us with an
expired httpcert.pem file.

Steve Timm

over the next day or two, that would be very useful.
