    Apr 16, 2018 10:24 PM UTC 
    Hi all,

Just FYI, I opened a JIRA ticket to track the software work for this: https://jira.opensciencegrid.org/browse/SOFTWARE-3221

Currently there is a new build of gratia-probe (1.20.1-1.osg34) available from osg-development.  This contains a fix to handle duration formats of either raw seconds or HH:MM:SS for the resources_used.walltime and resources_used.cput keys, as both were present in Erik's log sample and the old version of the pbs-lsf probe was only parsing the ones in HH:MM:SS format.

Feel free to yum upgrade your gratia-probe packages out of development for early access, otherwise this will go through our normal osg testing phase before it goes into a release.

Carl
