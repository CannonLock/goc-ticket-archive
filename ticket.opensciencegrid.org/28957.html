    Update on this ticket:
After reading the error message more carefully, it occurred to me that the error might be in my frontend collector condor config. I looked in there and found a file in /etc/condor/certs/condor_mapfile that contained a list of DN's that needed to be updated. I updated this and restarted condor on my schedd and collector frontends, and now the gwms-frontend service continues to run. I would like to leave this ticket open in case there might be other changes that will be required to complete the changeover to the new ca on my frontends.
