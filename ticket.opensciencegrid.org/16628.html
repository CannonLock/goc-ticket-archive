First (that would I believe also resolve the underlying ticket ( https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/viewer?id=16628)) , is that Role=Analysis for nova is not supported by their GUMS configuration. They will do the needful here.
Second, some certs work and some do not because the ones that work happen to belong to another group (users of which are fetched by their GUMS server)
