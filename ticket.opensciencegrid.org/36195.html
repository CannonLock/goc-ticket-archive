    Mar 5, 2018 09:28 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Yes, it was resolved.Yes, it was resolved.  The problem is that the Rucio deletion service host cache the DNS round robin of dcgftp.usatlas.bnl.gov.  Therefore, if one of the 18 hosts associated with dcgftp.usatlas.bnl.gov were down, and if the deletion host happen to use that host due to DNS caching, it will fail for a while until (a) DNS cache expires in the Rucio deletion service host or (b) the bring up the downed host.  The easiest thing to reduce the future error is to change the caching period in Rucio deletion service to be fairly short (say 10 minutes).    At BNL, we will look into the better way of doing round-robin.
