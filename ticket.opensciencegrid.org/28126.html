    Hi Eduardo,

Did you already run the following openssl commands?

To verify it's the new and not old cert:
openssl x509 -in /etc/grid-security/xrd/xrdcert.pem -enddate

To verify the cert and key are a match:
openssl x509 -in /etc/grid-security/xrd/xrdcert.pem -noout -modulus
openssl rsa -in /etc/grid-security/xrd/xrdkey.pem -noout -modulus

And verified the permissions are correct?

Thank you
