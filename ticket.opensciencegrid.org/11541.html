    Jan 27, 2012 09:25 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Since installing glite-CREAM-3.2.11-2, we have observed an issue a few times after coming out of an enforced downtime.  The error is that a pilot job comes in from the TRIUMF pilot factory and we see "lease time expired".

The behaviour was reproduced at TRIUMF by Di Qing with their new CREAM (ce2, base on glite-CREAM-3.2.11-2) by deleting information from the MySQL database.  The pyfactory remembered the old delegation id and no new jobs could be successfully submitted until the pyfactory queue was stopped and cleaned up.  This may be related in part to https://condor-wiki.cs.wisc.edu/index.cgi/tktview?tn=2351

Operationally this probably could be solved in the short term if the pilot factory queues were flushed for a site that went offline, but this operation may need to be conducted at multiple factories for some sites.  This problem does _not_ occur in glite-CREAM-3.2.10-0 so the suspicion is that this could be solved in CREAM and not require any CondorG modifications.
