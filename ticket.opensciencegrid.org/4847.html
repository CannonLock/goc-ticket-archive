    Apr 14, 2008 09:19 PM UTC by wcmaier@.... 
    Hi Scott-

I have a theory. At our site, we implement a wrapper around jobs
that allows them to run in our AFS environment. For jobmanager-condor,
this wrapper assumes that the job is transferring in the executable.
Your test should complete successfully if you ship in your own
/usr/bin/env (and leave transfer_executable set to true).

The assumption made in our wrapper may seem silly in context of your
test ("Why should I transfer a 4 kB executable?"). Most jobs (and
perhaps all 'real' jobs), however, send in some executable. In fact,
this is the first time that this corner case has been observed, and
the script has been in production for quite a while. ;)

For now, I suggest that you simply leave transfer_executable at its
default (true) setting -- I imagine you would do this anyway for
real jobs. When Dan returns from vacation, I'll work with him to
cover this case in our wrapper script. Thanks for bringing it to our
attention!

We'll track the fix to our wrapper script in our local help tracker;
if Scott agrees, I suggest this GOC ticket be closed.

Thanks again!
