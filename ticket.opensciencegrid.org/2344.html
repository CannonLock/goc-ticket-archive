I am a little surprised that you need a certificate to register a new VO with the OSG
so I've cc'd the OSG operations people to get the official and proper procedure to
follow here. My suspicion though is that what you technically need is a personal
certificate one that is issued by a known and trusted CA (Certificate Authority) such
as the DOEGrids. In the past a VO was not necessary to obtain one these. All that was
