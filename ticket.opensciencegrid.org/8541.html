    May 6, 2010 03:43 PM UTC by Arvind Gopu 
    Tony,

The only automated form we have to create a critical priority ticket is the BDII one if the answer to "Is the BDII responding?"  is no. This is intentional in that we don't want anyone to open critical priority ticket because they think an issue is critical as opposed to preset guidelines and agreed upon conditions.

The easiest way to bump up a ticket's priority is to call the GOC, and request that ticket NNNN be escalated to a higher priority. We considered adding text next to the priority listing on our ticket viewer but I don't recall exactly what we decided to do with that and/or when.

Cheers,
-ag
