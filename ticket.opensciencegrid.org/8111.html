    Feb 12, 2010 04:33 PM UTC by Arvind Gopu 
    Hi Jose,

First, are you familiar with the OIM topology? Have not checked out the OIM topology presentation on the home page? https://oim.grid.iu.edu/ .. If not, I request you go through it. It should only take a few minutes.

I will use terminology used in the topology below:

If I am reading your note correctly, you are trying to have 2 *resources* [i.e computers with an FQDN] running CEs and 1 *resource* running SE; and you want all of these to be in one *resource_group*. Correct?
If so then all you need to do as site-administrator is to map your two CE resources and one SE resource to one resource group .. I see you currently have two resource groups HEPGRID_UERJ and HEPGRID_UERJ_OSG64 within your site [geographic location of a department within a laboratory or university]

For further explanation, refer to this MyOSG view
http://tinyurl.com/ycunrsw

You can see how the USCMS-tier1 has one resource_group with 5 resources, 4 of which run CEs and 1 of which runs an SE; in your case one resource_group has a CE and an SE while the other one has just the 64-bit CE.
