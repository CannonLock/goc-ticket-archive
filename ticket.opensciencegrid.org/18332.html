    Dec 11, 2013 08:25 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi,

After the upgrade to CentOS 6.5 and OSG 3.2.0 the CE is not operating properly.
After the OpenSSL downgrade, all RSV metrics are green, but many users are complaining about submission errors.

From the user perspective, in the condor-g, the jobs enter in held just after the submission.
Further debugging, this issue also happens with direct globus-job-run commands (only to jobmanager-pbs, not to jobmanager-fork).

In the CE, there are a lot of segfaults of globus-job-manager process, like this:

Dec 11 18:09:45 ce kernel: globus-job-mana[4208]: segfault at 8 ip 0000003203881451 sp 00007fffc6d45cb8 error 4 in libc-2.12.so[3203800000+18b000]

And a lot of globus-job-manager "main" processes waiting, exhausting the CE memory, forcing the kernel to kill process.
All parent process are waiting for socket communication, like this:

[root@ce globus]# strace -p 6938
Process 6938 attached - interrupt to quit
connect(11, {sa_family=AF_FILE, path="/var/lib/globus/gram_job_state/vinicius/ce/pbs.da0d2a84.sock"}, 110

In the log:
[root@ce globus]# tail -f /var/log/globus/gram_vinicius.log
ts=2013-12-11T20:20:05.346255Z id=5949 event=gram.send_job.end level=WARN status=-3 errno=111 msg="Error making datagram connection to Job Manager" reason="Connection refused" path="/var/lib/globus/gram_job_state/vinicius/ce/pbs.da0d2a84.sock"
ts=2013-12-11T20:20:05.345645Z id=5263 event=gram.send_job.end level=WARN status=-3 errno=111 msg="Error making datagram connection to Job Manager" reason="Connection refused" path="/var/lib/globus/gram_job_state/vinicius/ce/pbs.da0d2a84.sock"
ts=2013-12-11T20:20:05.346317Z id=7177 event=gram.send_job.end level=WARN status=-3 errno=111 msg="Error making datagram connection to Job Manager" reason="Connection refused" path="/var/lib/globus/gram_job_state/vinicius/ce/pbs.da0d2a84.sock"

This may be related to the ticket https://jira.opensciencegrid.org/browse/SOFTWARE-1051, but is highly unlike that this is a hardware error. This is a real machine, not a VM.

Please advise,
Gabriel
