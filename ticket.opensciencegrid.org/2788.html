    As Dantong said, we should discuss the policy of escalating tickets.

As of now, if a site ignores a ticket we close it after a few weeks with annotation that we got no
response and it was not resolved. I prefer not to keep unowned tickets forever as they clog the
display and if there are to many unowned tickets then experts will have tendency to ignore tham
all.

What we can do is to move the tickets which were not acted upon within some time frame to status
"stalled". This will not close them, but will allow us to track better sites which do not react to
tickets.
