FootPrints Ticket Description:
I just confirmed that the records were not uploaded because of a preceding "*" in the description. We have a mechanism in place to retry failed uploads for a specific period of time before the error'ed out records are flagged as a permanent error'ed record. This is why I was not aware of this till now, in case you are wondering.

I am guessing the msg-publish tool requires that the first character of the description be an alphanumeric character? i am not sure. The specifications document we use does not give us a clear requirement, it just says the value must be a "string"
