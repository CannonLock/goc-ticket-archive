Hi,

Our cluster has been receiving very few OSG jobs
(gadu and grow only). We took a look in

http://osg-cat.grid.iu.edu/index.php?view=sitename&;whichmap=usasiasamerica&numentries=100&sortval=services&id=37

and it seems our $APP, $DATA and $TMP directories are
showing up as zero bytes. They are not zero - this
is happening because the OSG machine configures them
to a NFS-mounted volume in another machine (Raid
device). This machine's kernel is 2.4.21-20, which has
a bug that exports a volume by NFS just fine, but the
destination machine (OSG machine) reports it wrongly:

nas-0-0.local:/raid1    1      1     1     0%
/storage/raid1

Could this be the reason why we are receiving so few
jobs? Maybe the submitters see that $APP, $DATA and
$TMP directories are zero and give up submitting to
here because of that.

If so, please warn us quickly and we'll get to solve
this problem.

Thanks in advance,
The T2-HEPGRID UERJ team
