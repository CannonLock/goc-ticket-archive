my notes that NET2 had an *incomplete* replica and SWT2 had a *complete*
replica:

brunejo@egg:~$ dq2-list-dataset-replicas
group09.105802.JF17_pythia_jet_filter.recon.AOD.e347_a84_tid063927
INCOMPLETE: NET2_MCDISK
COMPLETE: BNL-OSG2_MCDISK,MWT2_UC_MCDISK,SWT2_CPB_MCDISK

It made no sense to us why the transfer was going in the opposite
direction, from NET2 -> SWT2.  The end result is that the errors were
expected, since we indeed didn't have the files.  We asked about this in
the DDM meeting, but we couldn't identify the reason behind the transfers.

Since the purpose of such transfers would've been to get the files to
SWT2, and SWT2 now has a complete replica of this dataset (as confirmed
by dq2-ls), and furthermore there was nothing unexpected at our site (we
did not have the files), I am closing this ticket.
