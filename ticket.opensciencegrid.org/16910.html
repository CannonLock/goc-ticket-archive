    Sep 10, 2013 08:28 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    I believe I found the cause for why the oasis repository was not picking up the
new revisions of the catalog.

I believe it was a combination of an old oasis configuration file (pre yum
install),
coupled with too small of a cache for oasis (only 1GB), coupled with a now very
large oasis catalog,
coupled with a bug in cvmfs 2.0.19 (and also 2.1.14) where catalog updates
cease if
the pinned catalog exceed 40% of the cache size.

I have fixed the configuration, increased the default cache to 10GB, and forced
a remount of the
repository on all nodes at MWT2. This was accomplished with

cvmfs-talk remount

and in some cases of very old catalogs

umount -l /cvmfs/oasis.opensciencedgrid.org

I have now seen the nodes update from revision 543 to 545 and now some nodes
are moving to 547.

I will monitor the nodes to make certain the revisions continue and the problem
resolved.

Secondarily, our few remaining SL5 nodes, on which we also run non-Atlas VO
jobs did not
have the oasis repository installed. Those nodes now have access to oasis
