    Jan 14, 2009 01:33 PM UTC by Arvind Gopu 
    Hi Iwona,

We're basing all our systems based on registration data in OIM. I've put
in some temporary fixes to the translator code (from CEMon LDIF to
BDII-ready LDIF) that ensures all 4 NERSC resources show up on the OSG
BDII.

See:
http://is1.grid.iu.edu/cgi-bin/status.cgi
http://is2.grid.iu.edu/cgi-bin/status.cgi

Do not worry about the fact that the CEMon file is not linked on the
above status pages as long as the BDII link is listed. Being in the BDII
is the ultimate goal, and CEMon is just the messenger :)

We'll put in a more permanent solution in place in the near future that
picks up possible aliases from OIM (we'll need admins to register the
aliases as well) to figure out what is a valid CEMon file, and what is
not. Stay tuned!

Cheers,
Arvind
