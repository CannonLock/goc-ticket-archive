fermigrid2.fnal.gov will be decommissioned no later than Friday
29-Feb-2008.

The replacement VOMS servers have already been commissioned on
voms.fnal.gov, and my VOMS monitoring scripts are reporting that
the following DNs are successfully performing voms-proxy-init's
against the nanohub VO VOMS server on voms.fnal.gov:

    
VODN
--------------
nanohub /CN=Invigo Service04/OU=Purdue TeraGrid/O=Purdue University/ST=Indiana/C=US
nanohub /CN=Invigo Service07/OU=Purdue TeraGrid/O=Purdue University/ST=Indiana/C=US

So, it appears that some nanohub VO scripts are referring to "stale" vomses files.

Please update *ALL* your vomses files as follows:

Replace:

"nanohub" "fermigrid2.fnal.gov" "15022" "/DC=org/DC=doegrids/OU=Services/CN=host/fermigrid2.fnal.gov"
"nanohub"

With:

"nanohub" "voms.fnal.gov" "15022" "/DC=org/DC=doegrids/OU=Services/CN=http/voms.fnal.gov" "nanohub"

Thank you.
-Keith.
