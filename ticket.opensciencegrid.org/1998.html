    Two 1G RAM modules were added today and a request was made to restart the backups.
    May 19, 2006 06:34 PM UTC by John Rosheck 
    I ran some comparison testing from grid02 to grid01 (which has identical hardware and
OS/distro) and these two machines performed almost identically.  I think this rules
out any hardware issues.  The same tests were also run on grid03 (identical hardware
but runs a 2.4 series kernel and Scientific Linux) and its numbers were significantly
worse than those of grid02 so it seems that moving grid02 to a 2.4 series kernel
would not help.  At this point, we are looking into things such as adding a better
RAID card, dividing the volumes and giving each its own SCSI channel.

I have proposed to Iosif that we move MonaLisa back to IU since CERN still seems to
have ongoing network problems.
