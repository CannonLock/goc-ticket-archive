    May 9, 2016 07:15 PM UTC by echism 
    Jake,

You may want to try making a new keypair.

backup the old files like so:
cd /home/jmichaelson/.globus/
mv usercert.pem usercert.pem.old
mv userkey.pem userkey.pem.old

make a new keypair like so:
openssl pkcs12 -in /home/jmichaelson/certs/user_certificate_and_key.U2930.p12 -out usercert.pem -nodes -clcerts -nokeys
openssl pkcs12 -in /home/jmichaelson/certs/user_certificate_and_key.U2930.p12 -out userkey.pem -nodes -nocerts
chmod 644 userkey.pem
chmod 400 userkey.pem
