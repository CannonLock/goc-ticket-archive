    Jan 8, 2013 08:19 PM UTC by Soichi Hayashi 
    Shaomin,

p12 file contains both your certificate and private key. You need both certificate and private key installed on your browser for your certificate to be useful.

OIM will remove your private key from memory once your session expires (for security reasons), so after while you can only download your certificate in pkcs7 format.

You can import your p12 file via your browser's certificate management function.
