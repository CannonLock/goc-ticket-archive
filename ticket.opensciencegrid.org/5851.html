    Oct 26, 2008 12:43 AM UTC by Fermilab 
    The Fermi/Remedy ticket has been updated.

New Work Log Entry : The following was e-mailed to the Requester:

timm@.... sent this Notes To Requester:
Hi Greg

fermigridosg1 is a front end to 8 different clusters at
Fermilab, seven of which share an $OSG_DATA of /grid/data,
and one, the uscms cluster, which does not.

You can restrict your jobs only to
the ones which share $OSG_DATA with fermigridosg1 by adding
the following RSL to your job

globusrsl = (gluerequirements=(requirements 'GlueCEInfoDataDir ==
\"/grid/data\"'))

Or you can also pre-stage your data, independently, to the
CMS cluster as well by means of gridftp to node cmsosgce3.fnal.gov,
that way it will be there no matter which way.

Let us know if you need more help or have trouble
with the syntax of the globusrsl statement.
There's more info at http://fermigrid.fnal.gov/matchmaking.html
