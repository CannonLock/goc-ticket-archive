    May 7, 2014 09:13 AM UTC 
    I had probably similar issue with certificate/key generated on SLF6. I summarized my findings in this bugreport

https://github.com/jglobus/JGlobus/issues/118

My problem was caused by unsupported private key format in jglobus package and I also found workaround

openssl rsa -in mykey.pem -out mykey.pem.old

This command on SLF6 converts mykey.pem in mykey.pem.old which has format supported by jglobus

(I could not follow official documentation, because OSG CA doesn't issue our certificates and we must use Terena eScience CA)

Petr
