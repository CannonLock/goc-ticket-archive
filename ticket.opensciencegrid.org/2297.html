Support Centers,

The VDT team has updated the CA certificates to reflect the contents of the IGTF 1.7
distribution.

To update your OSG 0.4.0 or 0.4.1 site please do the following.

# cd $VDT_LOCATION
# source setup.sh
# pacman -update CA-Certificates
