    Mar 18, 2008 06:35 PM UTC by Tim Silvers 
    Hi Alan,

VORS is displaying the data it captured from running the site_verify scan. Looking at the script,
site_verify is giving us the output of the contents of the $GLOBUS_LOCATION/etc/grid-services/
directory. So, looking at your node:

$ globus-job-run antaeus.hpcc.ttu.edu /bin/sh -c "ls /usr/local/OSG_0.8.0/globus/etc/grid-
services/"
jobmanager
jobmanager-condor
jobmanager-fork
jobmanager-lsf
jobmanager-managedfork

I thought that this was set when you run configure-osg.sh, but I could be wrong. I've looked at the
script and didn't see it. I know you're asked which job managers you support. And looking at my
ITB node the files in $GLOBUS_LOCATION/etc/grid-services/ are dated the day I setup and
configured the node. So I'd suggest simply running configure-osg.sh again. Alternatively, you could
probably just remove the condor file. BIt I'd like to see if configure_osg.sh will remove it. I look
forward to hearing back from you.
