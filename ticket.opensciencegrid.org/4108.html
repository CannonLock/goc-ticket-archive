New Work Log Entry : The following was e-mailed to the Requester:

timm@.... sent this Notes To Requester:

Hi Mats

We removed the OSG environment variables from jobs on fermigrid1,
both jobmanager-fork and jobmanager-condor.   fermigrid1 forwards
jobs to other subclusters here at FermiGrid and it was setting
a bunch of variables that were confusing jobs (including engage jobs)
once they got forwarded to the final destination. Our first attempt
at a fix was to strip them all out.

Our developer is now looking at a way to put the variables
back into the fork job manager.  It could be a couple of
days before we get it fixed.

Steve Timm
