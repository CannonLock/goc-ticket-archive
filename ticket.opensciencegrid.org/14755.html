    Hi, p.K,

"DOE Grids CA.1" sounds old to me. Which instructions are you using?

OSG uses Digicert as its Certificate Authority. I would suggest that you look here:

https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Documentation/CertificateGetWeb#Installing_into_a_Web_Browser

and get the Root and CA-1 files linked there. The filenames are "DigiCertGridRootCA.cer" and "DigiCertGridCA-1.cer". Once you've downloaded them, go back into your Certificate Manager window.

- Make sure the "Authorities" tab is selected at the top of the window.

- Click "Import" at the bottom of the window.

- Find and import the file DigiCertGridRootCA.cer.

- Click "Import" at the bottom of the window.

- Find and import the file DigiCertGridCA-1.cer

Then you should be able to click "Your Certificates" at the top of the Certificate Manager window and import the personal certificate you downloaded from OSG.

Let me know if you have further questions or this doesn't work for you.

Tom
