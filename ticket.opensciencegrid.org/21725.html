    Jul 9, 2014 08:25 PM UTC 
    Hi Becky,

Something is wrong.  GlideinWMS is designed to run on grid sites without proper reverse DNS lookup.  If you look at the error, it says a daemon with your submitter certificate is attempting to contact it.  No worker node in the world is going to have the same DNS entry as your submitter.

If I had to guess, I would say that your glideinwms setup is incorrect.  Your glideinwms submitter should explicitly allow the submission certificate you listed below to connect, otherwise, how will it every authenticate with the submitter?

I'm not sure of the GlideinWMS way to do this, but we use a condor_mapfile to map the glidein certificate (in your case, /DC=com/DC=DigiCert-Grid/O=Open Science Grid/OU=Services/CN=pilot/glidein2.chtc.wisc.edu ) to a recognized name.  Then ALLOW_DAEMON on that name.

Also, I'm surprised that you are seeing this in the shadowlog.  The shadows and startd should be communicating using the SEC_ENABLE_MATCH_PASSWORD_AUTHENTICATION = TRUE, which should bypass any GSI security.

-Derek
