    The only thing that I noticed that was different from what I was able to see on my own machine was that in the upper right hand of oim yours said no cert. My said login. From what I was able to find out about that is:

If you see above label on your OIM page, it could mean one of following.
You don't have any X509 Certificate installed on your browser.
You have X509 Certificate installed on your browser, but it's signed by CA that OIM does not trust.
You have X509 Certificate installed on your browser that OIM trusts, but you choose not to provide it.

Since I know none of that is true based on your screenshots. You might want to uninstall your cert and reinstall it into your browser then restart the machine and see if that clears the issue...
