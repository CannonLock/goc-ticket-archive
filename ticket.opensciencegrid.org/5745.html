    Oct 9, 2008 11:10 PM UTC by echism 
    Heidi,

I have activated your membership. As far as the other people you want
to register, you want to consider the following roles:

VO Manager- I think this is self explanatory. Infrequently used
contact, but an important one nonetheless.

Operations Contacts- Handle things like VOMS problems, membership
inquiries, administrative roles. They are the most frequently used
contacts and should be people who are very good about checking and
responding to email.

Security Contacts- Handle security problems. Due to the critical
nature of this work, these should be people who are comfortable with
being on-call in the rare case of an emergency.

Miscellaneous Contacts- People to contact when we cannot reach any of
the contacts in one particular group. You might want to give
preference to people with some versatility.

The more contacts, the better, in my experience, and though we want
you to tier the manager, security and operations contacts, you can
have as many miscellaneous contacts as you want. Does that answer your
question?

Thank you,
Elizabeth
