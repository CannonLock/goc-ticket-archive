> The weekly and monthly averages are done by applying the above

> The table shows the daily, weekly, monthly, and the month to date

> the month through the first day of the report.  Weekly is 7 days not
> including the previous day.
>
> The following tests are marked critical:
> - version
> - ce perm
> - ping
> - gram
> - ca cert
>
> This report uses the WLCG coloring scheme:
> - 90-100% is marked with "green"
> - 60-90% is marked with "yellow"
> - 30-60% is marked with "orange"
> - 0-30% is marked with "red"
>
> An asterisk (*) next to a site name indicates a site reporting to
> the WLCG, but not in the MoU.
> All WLCG MoU sites are printed in the first table.
> Each row corresponds to an individual site reporting in RSV; WLCG
> federations are not grouped.
