    Dear FNAL admins,

We would like to run some staging tests for your site in the preparation of Run2. The general idea is to transfer large datasets from your tape to your disk endpoint. The subscriptions will be made to the PhEDEx DataOps group, so the space will automatically be created, and the datasets will be removed upon completion.
More info can be found at the following twiki:
https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/CMSPublic/CompOpsRun2T1StagingTests

The list of datasets is in [1]. Could you please flush these from your Buffer so that the data starts transferring from tape?
If possible, we would also like to ask for some internal monitoring plots during the duration of the test. In particular, it will be useful to see the rate of tape->buffer and buffer->disk.

In addition, T0->T1 debug transfers have been started at the end of last week. The (ongoing) results are available here:
https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/CMSPublic/CompOpsRun2T0T1TransferTestsMarch2017
The table header for each endpoint is the targeted rate, and the plot shows the transfer rate along the debug link since the start of the test.

Currently at FNAL, the MSS target rate of 1054 MB/s, so far tests are getting as a results, an average rate of 684 MB/s and a pick rate of 1455 MB/s. On the other side, for the Disk endpoint, having in average 684 MB/s for a target rate of 2108 MB/s. In order to better prepare for the Run2, we would like to take a look at the performance for both endpoints.

Regards,
Sebastian.
Transfer Team
