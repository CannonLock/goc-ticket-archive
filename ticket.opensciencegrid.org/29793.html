    May 12, 2016 09:15 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi All,

The correct way to send an EGI broadcast is through:
https://operations-portal.egi.eu/broadcast, the mailing list your want is "site administrators" and then pick the countries that support LZ.
To first order you need to inform the administrators rather than the users as they rarely overlap. You might have to talk to the portal people to get the correct permissions to be able to send these broadcasts. I've searched my inbox and there was no warning sent to any of the sites.

There is no central authority for the non-US grid sites that updates server certificates, so we rely on the portal (we automatically pull information from there).
The Operations Portal pulls some information from the from the voms server, but I don't think it can update the DN automatically (that would be quite a security hole, no ?). Looking at the LZ listing on the Operations Portal, it's still not right (the query to the vomsserver fails). I think right now only Tapas has access to it (http://operations-portal.egi.eu/vo/search -> search for lz, I don't think I can make a direct link). My understanding is that Tapas has left, so you need to talk to the Operations people to update this, or maybe Tapas can add someone to the VO by hand. Then can you please update the portal, otherwise this will drag on forever.

On a  slightly related note: You seem to maintain a vomsserver just for the lz VO with 14 members. That seems like overkill to me. If you wanted to get rid of it, we could host the VO in the UK. We have a set of three redundant vomsservers in three different cities (https://voms.gridpp.ac.uk:8443/) that host about 30+ VOs (including one in Canada and in Japan), and we'd be happy to add lz to these. (This does not affect who is the VO manager etc.)

Finally I've sent out information to the UK sites, is:
/DC=org/DC=opensciencegrid/O=Open Science Grid/OU=Services/CN=voms.hep.wisc.edu
/DC=org/DC=cilogon/C=US/O=CILogon/CN=CILogon OSG CA 1
(ports etc are all unchanged, only voms server cert is changed)

I think this is correct, but if not, can you please let me know ASAP.
