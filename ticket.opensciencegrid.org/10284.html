    Apr 26, 2011 03:41 PM UTC by Steven Timm 
    It's not in the version that I just downloaded 5 minutes
ago from
http://software.grid.iu.edu/pacman/tarballs/vo-package/gums.template

Right at the top of the file you will see that
voms.fnal.gov is consulted for cdf:/cdf/glidecaf/Role=development,
cdf:/cdf/glidecaf/Role=fermigrid
cdf:/cdf/glidecaf/Role=namcaf
cdf:/cdf/glidecaf/role=testcaf
but *not*
cdf:/cdf

The other two voms servers (voms.cnaf.infn.it and voms01.pd.infn.it have
groups like
cdf-cnaf or cdf-pd
that do map the cdf:/cdf role.

It's been this way forever, we just discovered it last week
because the other two servers were not in order.

So a group should be added that looks like this in gums1.1-speak:
----------------------------------------------------
<groupMapping name="cdf-fnal" accountingVo="cdf" accountingDesc="CDF">
<userGroup className="gov.bnl.gums.VOMSGroup"
url="https://voms.fnal.gov:8443/voms/cdf/services/VOMSAdmin";
persistenceFactory="mysql" name="cdf-fnal" voGroup="/cdf"
sslCertfile="/etc/grid-security/http/httpcert.pem"
sslKey="/etc/grid-security/http/httpkey.pem" matchFQAN="exact"/>
<accountMapping className="gov.bnl.gums.GroupAccountMapper"
groupName="cdf"/>
</groupMapping>

-------------------------------------------------
