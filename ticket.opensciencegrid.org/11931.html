    Hi,

I'm not sure this is an RACF or US ATLAS issue, as it seems to be related to
the ESnet/DOEGrids certificate application process, a problem with which I too
experienced earlier this week. Although Michael mentioned that this issue was
fixed, and my most recent attempt to renew my certificate did complete with a
successful request, my browser (Firefox 11) still erred on the renewed
certificate afterward instead of automatically importing it, due to what it
reported as a CA problem [see attached].

I suspect the current instructions to download the latest CA chain are not
enough: when importing the chain, users will be prompted that the import is not
necessary, since the CA chain already exists and remains valid. Instead, users
will need to manually _delete_ the DOEGrids _and_ ESnet chains from their
browsers and other key stores, and then re-import the new version.

Alden: can you please try this, and then retry your renewal request?

As an aside, regarding the topic of administration: this message appears at the
bottom of each such GOC communication:
> Primary Admin for ATLAS VO is Dantong Yu and has been CC'd.

Dantong is no longer with our facility and should not be CCed regarding such
issues. In fact, there is no need to CC any individual admin or user, as issues
to our ticket system are routed to the appropriate queue and thereby reach the
appropriate admins. CCing Dantong or any other individual only results in the
delivery of multiple copies of the same communication.

Thanks,
~John
