The hostGroups has to change engage-ucsdgrid is evaluated before engage:
"..., engage-uscsgrid, engage, ..."
This order is critical.  If not changed, the engage-ucsdgrid members will map
to the engage group account first due to the matchFQAN='vo' criteria.

Members of the engage-ucsdgrid group always have to use a voms
proxy with that specific FQAN.

All other engage members can use voms proxy specifying any group/subgroup,
and also use grid proxies (assuming they are not members of other VOs which
allow grid proxies).
