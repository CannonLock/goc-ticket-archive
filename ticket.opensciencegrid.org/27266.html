    Hi Armen,

okay, I finally figured this out. The error was completely incorrect, as I
suspected. The actual problem was that the certificates area was out of date on
the SE, since the server had crashed with a kernel oops while I was out of the
country (around Oct 14), and I had temporarily commented out the hourly CA
syncing in order to avoid getting an hourly error email while there was nothing
I could do about it. After rebooting the server, it came back up fine, but I
forgot to put the CA syncing back in place, hence the CERN CA CRL files weren't
updated, and that caused bestman to refuse to talk to CERN servers.

This would've been MUCH easier to figure out if I had gotten the ACTUAL error,
namely 'CA expired', rather than this bogus 'connection reset by peer', which
was not what happened at all!

That java error must've been quite old, too, since that only happens during
initial bestman startup, which hasn't happened since I restarted it last week.

Anyway, I'm closing this ticket again now.

Cheers,

Horst
