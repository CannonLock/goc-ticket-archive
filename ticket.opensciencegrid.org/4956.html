The problem had to do with jobs failing at CIT because a combination of
held grid jobs and ReSS sent thousands of local jobs to CIT when there
were less than a dozen batch slots.  Many jobs were failing and this
prompted the ticket by CIT.

In essence this is the same issue as the abitibi.sbgrid.org ticket.  In
response to that one (and this one),  I am not submitting jobs via ReSS
to sites with few batch slots available to DZero.  I am sending jobs to
them using the dumb algorithm in automc.  However this does not
guarantee a site will not be flooded with jobs since a rash of grid jobs
being held will cause the same effect as bad monitoring.  As far as I am
concerned this ticket can be closed since the ReSS issues are on the
table.  DZero jobs do run at CIT albeit with the disappointing
efficiency seen at other sites.  thanks.

    Hi Michael,

Submissions to CIT are handled by ReSS to a pool of sites.  I have no
control over the jobs sent there.  As I mentioned earlier jobs are
submitted in sets with up to 800 jobs in a set.  If ReSS decides to send

available then the algorithm needs tuning.  Perhaps the ReSS people should
comment on this. thanks.
