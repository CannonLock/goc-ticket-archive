The commands to generate the voms credentials, store them at BNL and check on
them are:

1. make your voms proxy with 96 hours
voms-proxy-init -valid 96:0 -voms atlas:/atlas

2. Upload your proxy by
glite-delegation-init -f -s
https://fts02.usatlas.bnl.gov:8443/glite-data-transfer-fts/services/gridsite-delegation

3. Check if it is uploaded by
glite-delegation-info -v -s
https://fts02.usatlas.bnl.gov:8443/glite-data-transfer-fts/services/gridsite-delegation
