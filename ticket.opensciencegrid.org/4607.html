Dear Engage VO and Sebastien Goasguen,

Earlier today, I spent some time reviewing the Fermilab Site Authorization (SAZ)
service logs for repeated "authorization failures", and I noticed several jobs
that were submitted by Sebastien Goasguen, which fell into this category.  An
example is:

2008-02-12 04:00:50 :INFO :Thread-271116: Received SAZclient authorization request from 131.225.107.31 : fermigrid1.fnal.gov
2008-02-12 04:00:50 :INFO :Thread-271116: VO NULL
2008-02-12 04:00:50 :INFO :Thread-271116: Role NULL
2008-02-12 04:00:50 :INFO :Thread-271116: User /DC=org/DC=doegrids/OU=People/CN=sebastien goasguen 192255
2008-02-12 04:00:50 :INFO :Thread-271116: Cert /DC=org/DC=DOEGrids/OU=Certificate Authorities/CN=DOEGrids CA 1
2008-02-12 04:00:50 :INFO :Thread-271116: Sending to client N

Looking at the above output, it appears that Sebastien is submitting his grid

    job with only a grid-proxy-init generated proxy.  Fermilab policy is to require
that all jobs are submitted with a voms-proxy-init generated proxy:

http://fermigrid.fnal.gov/policy.html

Examination of our GUMS database, shows that Sebastien is only a member of the
engage VO:

mysql -u root GUMS_1_1 -e "select * from USER where DN like '%goasguen%'"
+--------+------------+------------------------------------------------------------+---------+
| ID     | GROUP_NAME | DN | FQAN    |
+--------+------------+------------------------------------------------------------+---------+
| 180062 | engage     | /DC=org/DC=doegrids/OU=People/CN=sebastien goasguen 192255 | /Engage |
+--------+------------+------------------------------------------------------------+---------+

In order for Sebastien to successfully submit jobs to Fermilab, he will need
to modify his grid job submission as follows:

replace

grid-proxy-init

with

voms-proxy-init -voms engage:/engage -userconf $HOME/vomses/vomses.engage

Where the contents of $HOME/vomses/vomses.engage should contain the appropriate
contact information for the engage VO voms server.

Thanks.
-Keith Chadwick.
