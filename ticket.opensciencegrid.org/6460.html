    Pablo,

We need a few things to complete your Rice registration. They are as follows:

- a site name: this is typically the name of the cluster where your resource is
installed, but any name is okay.
- a facility name: the name of your institution or organization.
- what support center, if any, you intend to support your resource (ie LIGO, USCMS,
USATLAS)
- your longitude and latitude so we can display your site on our map.
- we need to you pass site_verify.pl once before we activate Rice. You are currently
failing authentication and this typically happens when the MIS VO is not enabled or
the host/service certificates are not in place.
- Please attend our weekly operation call
http://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/Minutes2009March09
