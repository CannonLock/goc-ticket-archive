    Aug 28, 2007 10:20 PM UTC by Chris Green 
    Hi,

There appears to be a discrepancy in the VORS reporting at your site: VORS reports your OSG version as 0.4.1, but your VDT version is 1.6.1b, which only came out with OSG 0.6.0.

You *should* be able to:

cd $VDT_LOCATION
. setup.sh
pacman -get Gratia-SGE-Probe
configure_gratia --probe-cron --probe sge --report-to gratia.opensciencegrid.org:8881

If this doesn't work, they may be some problem related to the discrepancy between your OSG and VDT versions.

Thanks for your help,
Chris Green (Gratia Ops).
