'''

We consulted with the WMAgent developers and they let us know that the WMCore code had been changed to require the phedex-node tag for both the local and fallback stage out.

Please, add the phedex-node that to the fallback-stage-out section of your site-local-config.xml to avoid these errors in the future. As your fallback-stage-out is in T2_US_Florida, I'd suggest to set the value as it is set on their site-local-config.
