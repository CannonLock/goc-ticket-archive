    Hi Elizabeth --

I finally realized I could just openssl the pem certificate and key that
allow me to use dq2 just fine, back into p12 reload them into "Keychain" on
my Mac!!  So my browser works again!!  Winning command:

openssl pkcs12 -in userkey.pem -nocerts -export -out jamie_grid.p12 -name
"Jamie Grid"

In the past, I've found all of the VOMS memberships to be a bit finnicky,
so this is definitely easier than re-subscribing everything.

Thanks very much for all of your help!!!

Jamie
