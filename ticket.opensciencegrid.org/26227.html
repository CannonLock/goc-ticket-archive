    Hi Julian,

we brainstormed a little bit about this problem. We are having this problem since some time so I will try to clarify the situation in order to be able to converge to a solution. Please let me know if our understanding is correct.

The symptoms you sent us are:
1. the files are being staged out at UCSD.
2. the srmcp copy to UCSD fails.

There are a couple of questions here:
1. is production deliberately to stage out at UCSD? I do not see why, it would make more sense to stage out at our local storage.
2. If the transfers are going to UCD there could be at least 2 reasons:
a. the stageout to Caltech itself failed so production uses UCSD as a fallback. We do not see any errors at Caltech itself from the logs.
b. there is some confusion in the config of our site or of wmagent hence the stageout mistakenly goes to UCSD.

As far as the symptoms themselves go, I wrote before here that my impression is that the directory structure at UCSD is not created prior to trying to copy the file there with srmcp. As I remember, srmcp will not create the directory structure itself but srmmkdir commands need to be run for that.

Dorian.
