    Hi Andrew,

Could you please enable HTTPS support at Vanderbilt for your AAA endpoints.

You are getting this ticket because you have not listed Vanderbilt as having HTTPS access in this table:

https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/CMSPublic/USCMSTier2Upgrades

and it is one of the USCMS yearly goals.

Because this blocks other improvements (HTTPS support allows us to progress with GridFTP replacement and to integrate with CVMFS), we are starting to file tickets for the remaining sites in order to track the completion.

I have recently updated the documentation page:

https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/Main/XRootDoverHTTP

That should include more configuration snippets and some tests to perform to verify your setup.

Please let me know when you think things are working and I (or Marian) can validate.  Alternately, please let us know of any lacking documentation or technical problems.

Thanks!

Brian
