    Jun 30, 2008 08:20 PM UTC by echism 
    Hi Britta,

As an alternative to the condor_status, you can use the ldapsearch though the output
though may not as intuitive as condor_status commands.
Here is an example invocation (against UCSD site), all you need to change the -b option.

% ldapsearch -x -LLL -h is.grid.iu.edu -p 2170 -b
mds-vo-name=UCSDT2,mds-vo-name=local,o=grid "GlueCEAccessControlBaseRule=VO:ligo"
"GlueCEStateStatus=Production" GlueCEInfoHostName GlueCEStateFreeJobSlots
GlueCEStateTotalJobs GlueCEStateFreeCPUs GlueCEStateWaitingJobs

Going through the sites from your list, I verified that they all claim to support
ligo, so you should not be getting empty results from ReSS. On further investigation
I found two kinds of failure

1. Site not reporting to CEMon collector (NERSC_PDSF, ORNL_NSTG). For these site the
LDAP command will give you the following output.

No such object (32)
Matched DN: mds-vo-name=local,o=grid

We should open GOC tickets against these sites.

2. The rest of the sites do not appear to have classads in ReSS though reporting
appropriately to the CEMon collector and the information you are requesting can be
queried using ldapsearch. So you could use temporarily use ldapsearch command (as
identified above) to find the relevant information but I don't see any reason why the
class ads from these sites are missing. I am adding the ReSS developers (Parag and
Tanya) to the CC list, get their comments.

Let me know if you have any questions.

Thanks,
Anand
