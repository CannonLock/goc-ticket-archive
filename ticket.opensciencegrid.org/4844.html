Hello and Welcome to the Open Science Grid! Thank you for registering the gin.ggf.org Virtual
Organization. By completing the VO Registration form you have started the approval process to
have your VO added to the OSG. A detailed flowchart of the approval process can be found at
https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/StandardOperatingProcedures. This process should
take approximately 7 business days to complete.

Your VO will be presented for approval to the OSG Council on the first Thursday after it is
submitted and then sent to the next Operations Workgroup for discussion on the next Monday. If
you would like to attend the Operations Workgroup meeting to represent your VO and inform the
community of your participation and goal in joining the OSG you can find the details at
https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/ProductionMeetingMinutes.

There are several mailing lists associated with the OSG, as a new VO you have been subscribed to
the following mailing lists:

osg@.... -- For general OSG announcements

osg-users@.... -- for OSG Users Group and Meeting

osg-general@.... -- For general technical discussion and open community support

osg-operations@.... -- Discussion of operations activities and issues

osg-ops-status@.... -- Daily OSG Status Reports

    
vo-admins@.... -- Discussion of OSG VO and GUMS Administration

Details for subscribing to these lists and a list of other OSG mailing lists you may be interested in
subscribing to can be found at http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000089.

Please contact the Grid Operations Center (GOC) at goc@.... if you have any
questions.

Thank you for your interest in the OSG, and we look forward to your participation in the OSG
Consortium!

OSG Operations Workgroup
