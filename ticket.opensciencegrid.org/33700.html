GOCTX Source: https://ggus.eu/ws/ticket_info.php?ticket=128187
    May 6, 2017 03:10 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi,

This error "451 FTP proxy did not shut down" means that the door was
waiting for adapter to finish after the transfer-finished message
arrived, but finally timed out after 5 minutes.  I checked the logs in
recent days, this kind of error existed all the time but became a
little
bit higher since yesterday noon and distributed equally on all GFTP
doors.  Although we did make a change on most GFTP doors by installing
an updated version yesterday, it did not seem related since we had two
GFTP doors which kept the old version, but still have a little bit
higher occurrence of this "FTP proxy did not shut down", same as
others.

It seems to me the error was more likely caused by network
fluctuation.
Since at this time, the number of this error is very minor compared to
total number of successful transfers, we will keep an eye on it and
see.

Regards,
Jane
