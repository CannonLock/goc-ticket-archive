-- by Jacobo Tarragón Cros at Mon Oct 14 11:45:52 UTC 2013

Hi Chris, Horst,

the downtime did not propagate to the site level since the service tier2-02.ochep.ou.edu was in critical status but not covered under the scheduled downtime. Following the computation algorithm [1], the downtime is only propagated if other service types are either OK or CRITICAL.

Looking at the availability, it may seem that the downtime was indeed propagated the 7th. This is due to tier2-02 being in unknown state rather than critical for a fraction of the day.

I can perform a recomputation in case the downtime was meant to cover the SRMv2 service too, which is what caused the drop in the reliability. Was this the case?

Cheers,
Jacobo.

[1] https://wiki.egi.eu/wiki/Availability_procedure_improvements (section 4.4)
