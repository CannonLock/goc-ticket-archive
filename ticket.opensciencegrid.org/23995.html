    Feb 11, 2015 05:00 PM UTC by Carl Edquist 
    There was a good amount of follow-up discussion on the osg-software list about this.  The issue ended up being due to 2 duplicate (MAP,DN) pairs in the MAPPING table that differed only in the case (capitalization) in the DN.  (I'm pasting the summary from Steve Timm of how it happened below.)  After Neha deleted the two duplicate records, she was able to generate osg-user-vo-map.txt successfully.

I'm going to close out this ticket as the problem on Neha's installation is resolved.

I created the following jira tickets for gums improvements related to the issue:

https://jira.opensciencegrid.org/browse/SOFTWARE-1784
https://jira.opensciencegrid.org/browse/SOFTWARE-1785
https://jira.opensciencegrid.org/browse/SOFTWARE-1786

Thanks Steve, Brian, and Neha for the help debugging this!

Carl

--- From Steve ---

How we got here:
"Gerard Bernabeu altayo" joins fermilab VO--
his matching robot certs with lower case also added to his user in fermilab VO,

mapping table maps those 2 to pool accounts--that is persistent..entries
don't go away.

Gerard gets the capitalization of his name fixed
"Gerard Bernabeu Altayo" now is in the Fermilab VO.
2 more robots certs with the new capitalization also get made
and entered into gums mapping table as distinct entries.

-----------

GUMS should have recognized that lower and upper case Altayo
were the same DN, and not added it to the mapping table again. It didn't.
Yet based on string compares once they are in the table, it does
think they are the same.. weird.


    Fermilab VO has to do some work on our voms loading script for sure,
independent of what goes on here.
As to where the rest of the fixes happen--that is up to you guys
but I think I agree with what Brian said below that statements
that begin with "alter table" should be avoided if they can.
