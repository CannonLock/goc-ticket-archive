    Thank you for registering the cinvestav Resource. Your registration will be added to the OSG
Resource Structure.

As a registered OSG resource you should have a representative attend the weekly OSG Operations
Meeting to review issues facing your site and the OSG community.  The meetings are every Monday
at 1:00pm EST.  Please attend this meeting, especially following your registration.

The TWiki page for this meeting is: http://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/
ProductionMeetingMinutes

There are several mailing lists associated with the OSG, as a new Resource you may want to
subscribe to the following mailing lists:

osg@....  -- For general OSG announcements
osg-general@....  -- For general technical discussion and open community support
osg-operaions@....  -- Operations activities
osg-ops-status@....  -- OSG Status Reports

Details for subscribing to these lists and a list of other OSG mailing lists you may be interested in
subscribing to can be found at http://www.opensciencegrid.org/?pid=1000089.

Please contact the Grid Operations Center (GOC) at goc@.... if you have any
questions.

Thank you for registering your resource, and we look forward to your participation in the OSG!
