    Nov 16, 2006 04:36 PM UTC by Horst Severini 
    Hi Marco,

are your compute nodes on a private network?

I actually tried something similar here at OU_OCHEP_SWT2 yesterday,
and found out that globus-url-copy from a compute node behind a private
network will not work with multiple streams (-p 10) -- at least not for
inbound transfers, see below -- since the NAT can't deal with it apparently,
presumably because it involves inbound connections which aren't properly
forwarded by the NAT.

globus-url-copy with a single stream, i.e., without the -p option,
works fine, though.

Actually, outbound globus-url-copy transfers seem to work with parallel
streams, too -- presumably because they don't involve incoming connections,
since all the parallel streams are outbound? -- but I guess they're not
that common from compute nodes.

Hope this helps,

Horst
