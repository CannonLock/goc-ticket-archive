    Horst Severini reports, and I have confirmed, that this works.  He
reports as well, and I have confirmed at AGLT2, that the "--key"
argument is not needed.

The winning syntax is
curl --cert $X509_USER_PROXY --cacert $X509_USER_PROXY --capath
$X509_CERT_DIR <URL>

I am satisfied, and this ticket can be closed.

    Hi,

This is simply a change in curl in RHEL6; it uses a different SSL library which does not support proxies.  There's a workaround documented here:

https://wiki.nikhef.nl/grid/Funny_Curly_things#Conclusions

OSG doesn't ship curl as it is provided by RHEL.

Not much we can do here -- you may want to report to the upstream PanDA folks.

Brian
