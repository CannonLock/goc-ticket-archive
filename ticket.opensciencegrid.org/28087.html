    Jan 5, 2016 05:50 PM UTC by Steven Timm 
    The DN that Tanya put in this ticket must be registered in the OIM Contacts section

otherwise it does not have the right to read myosg.grid.iu.edu.

The gratia reporting has to read myosg.grid.iu.edu to get the list of resource contacts

and VO contacts to which to send out the reports.  Without the DN being registered, that

request will fail.  That's what has been happening for the last 4 months and that

is why people are not getting their VO and resource reports.

There is already a "Gratia" contact in OIM.. the DN has to be added to that as another allowed DN.
Which is what Nick Peregonow asked you to do 4 months ago and you did not do.

Steve
