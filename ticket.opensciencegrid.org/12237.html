    Jun 19, 2012 10:48 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Richard--OK that answers the question.
When glideins are sent to fermigridosg1 they can be forwarded to any number of
sub-clusters including cmsosgce3.fnal.gov.  That cluster is very busy so there
are rarely any free slots for gluex there, but our monitoring shows that there
were a few slots on the times that you saw the blocks.

Glidein factory should leave its blacklist of cmsosgce3.fnal.gov up for now because
that cluster still doesn't have a squid server available for general use, which means
you trigger the auto-blocker.  We have contacted
the admins of that cluster and asked them to add the squid, we will see what happens.
