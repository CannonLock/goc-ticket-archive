Hi Wayne,

The CE part is a red-herring — ignore all the condor-ce stuff.  Your schedd can’t talk to the cluster’s condor_collector/negotiator.

How’s the GSI setup within the condor cluster?  You should have a condor mapfile setup (different from /etc/grid-security/grid-mapfile or /etc/condor-
ce/condor_mapfile) that had the old hostcert in it.

Brian
