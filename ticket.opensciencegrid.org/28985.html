    Mar 18, 2016 06:58 PM UTC by Dave Dykstra 
    Log messages can be reduced by adding "negative-timeout=600" in the options section of the cvmfs line in /etc/auto.master.  That will prevent the automounter from trying to mount again for 10 minutes after it gets any error mounting any cvmfs repository.  The default is 60 seconds.
