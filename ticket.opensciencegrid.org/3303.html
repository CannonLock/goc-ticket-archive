    Thank you for the report.  We are the correct support center for our
site.  USCMS's policy is odd, in my view, in that they should simply
pass on support requests for Tier-3 centers to the appropriate site,
rather than reject them.

As for the particular problem encountered, this was apparently a
result of one of our system administrator's attempts to create a
special queue for gridex jobs to run in that had the undesired side-
effect of interfering with walltime specifications for normal user
jobs.  This has been fixed, so now walltime specifications should
work with queue unspecified.  Can Noam please test this to verify
that it works for his jobs?

Thanks for your patience while we worked this out.
