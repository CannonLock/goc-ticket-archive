    Feb 24, 2011 09:05 PM UTC by Terrence Martin 
    So here is the problem I think, we do not support a directory at UCSD that
allows read and write from all worker nodes. We advertise it still because of
a bug that existed in older installs of the OSG software that prevented us
from turning off OSG_DATA and setting it to a we do not support value.

That bug is gone in newer versions I believe, but I have not updated/set the
configs on my CEs.

Unfortunately I think your problems are as intended at UCSD. We can offer of
course hadoop access. Read from the mount point but writing to hadoop requires
srm. We do not support a shared POSIX like file system that mounts on all of
the worker nodes anymore.

Terrence
