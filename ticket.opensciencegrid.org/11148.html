    Oct 21, 2011 02:57 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Update on our problem today:

We have recovered from the problem causing Adler checksum mismatches and DDM
and Panda job errors. The files were not actually corrupted, but there was an
error where GPFS did not read the contents of some files correctly. Once the
reading problem was fixed, the files are no longer corrupted. Right now, we're
running checks and removing any extended Adler32 attributes from files modified
in the past three days to be sure that no bad checksums got into extended
attributes. This will take overnight to run. Assuming no further problems we
expect to start up DDM and our Panda queues tomorrow. - Saul & John
