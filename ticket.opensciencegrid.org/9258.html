    Sep 21, 2010 08:29 PM UTC by GRATIA 
    The Fermi/Remedy ticket has been resolved with the following solution.  Please resolve your ticket.

Resolution Categorization Tier 1 :
Resolution Categorization Tier 2 :
Resolution Categorization Tier 3 :
Resolution                       : During the network outage on 9/16, the gratia collector systems were restarted.  During the restart, housekeeping was re-enabled on gratia-osg-transfer.  This has been disabled for many, many months while we wait on new code from the developer.  Since it was re-enabled, it attempted to remove many backlogged events which caused database replication to fall severely behind (since it's single threaded).  Housekeeping has been once again disabled on gratia-osg-transfer, however the replica database must still continue on it's course until it catches up.  As such, we have failed the reporting database IP over to the collector db until the reporting db catches up in the next few days.
Resolution Method                :
