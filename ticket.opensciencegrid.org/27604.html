    We had a power outage for half of the datacenter where T2_US_Nebraska lives on the 26th. Took until later on the 27th as it was Thanksgiving day + icy conditions everywhere to get everything back. The CEs certainly all died, but various worker nodes survived, and during the recovery any other combination of new starting jobs + old running jobs finishing + etc… were certainly possible, so my guess is that this issue was simply a result of this outage. Appropriate mails were sent, at least to the lists I know of, when this happened on the 26th for the record.

If everything seems good at this point, I would say this can be closed.
