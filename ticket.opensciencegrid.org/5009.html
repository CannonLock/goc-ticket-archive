> hours (request column).

As I've learned by following the discussion between the BNL team and the
Globus developers there is no such thing as a maximum runtime for Condor
jobs as we are used to from other queuing systems. A user's jobs can
specify how long they _want_ to run (2 hours in our case on BNL) but
they may get suspended in between so that their total lifetime is not
limited. Please correct me if I'm wrong here.

BTW: Thanks to Xin I've resumed regular job submission to BNL_ATLAS_1.
My jobs seem to run fine again.
