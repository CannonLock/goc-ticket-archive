    Mar 29, 2018 12:47 AM UTC by Dave Dykstra 
    You must somehow not have a cvmfs-config-osg version that is new enough.  There should be a /etc/cvmfs/default.d/60-osg.conf that sets a CVMFS_CONFIG_REPOSITORY and a few other settings which will eventually get you a setting for CVMFS_SERVER_URL.   The lack of CVMFS_SERVER_URL is why you're getting 'malformed URL'.  Did you follow the instructions at https://opensciencegrid.github.io/docs/worker-node/install-cvmfs/ ?  Installing 'osg-oasis' should get you the correct cvmfs-config-osg rpm.

    Dan,

To answer your question at the end of your ticket description, running a frontier-squid-2 version still should be fine for the latest O.S. and cvmfs versions.

Try temporarily putting CVMFS_DEBUGLOG=/tmp/cvmfsdebug.log in /etc/cvmfs/default.local and attempting to mount again, and then attach /tmp/cvmfsdebug.log.  Note that the automounter only attempts to mount once every 5 minutes so sometimes you need to restart it in order to test again.
