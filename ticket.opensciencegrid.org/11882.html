Hello,

Please forward through ggus to CERN-PROD egi resource.

It has come to our attention that the long paths of the working directory on the worker nodes at CERN are causing issues for ATLAS glideins.
We are submitting to:
https://ce203.cern.ch:8443/ce-cream/services/CREAM2 lsf grid_atlas
https://ce208.cern.ch:8443/ce-cream/services/CREAM2 lsf grid_atlas

Currently the resulting CWD is of the form:
/home/grid/atlplt10/home_cream_261295296/CREAM261295296/glide_E18728/execute/dir_30426

The atlas admins have requested coming up with a solution that will give shorter paths, something like:
/home/grid/atlplt10/glide_E18728/execute/dir_30426

I believe /home/grid/atlplt10/home_cream_261295296/CREAM261295296/ is simply the CWD that the pilot job lands on.  We have tested running at $TMPDIR in the past at CERN and found this not to be reliable.  Are there any alternative ways you may suggest for us to shorten the paths?

Thanks,
Jeff Dost
OSG Glidein Factory Operations
