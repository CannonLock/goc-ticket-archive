    Dec 27, 2011 03:17 AM UTC 
    I am looking at the current week's unknown VO report.
This is what I know about some of the more common unknown VO's.
grid14,grid16,grid17 are all artifacts of a long-standing bug in the urCollector
scripts that are used for Gratia accounting in PBS (and presumably LSF).
If you see vo's like cms0,cms1,cms2 those are caused by the same Bug.
Tanya has a patch to fix this, it's currently in discussion with the VDT on
how to get it out there.

Engage could be fixed by a auto-lowercasing of all vo's as they come in, we have
a request into the gratia developers to this effect.
"map" is a sub-vo of the Fermilab VO which we thought we had registered.. there
is an unknown "feature" of OIM in which some vo's are declared inactive for
no good reason and I don't know how to fix the OIM entries correctly.
enmr is a group of Engagement.
superbvo.org is an anomaly in the GUMS template as it was put out from the GOC
when that VO was added to OSG.

Independently of fixing the individual unknown VO's, which all told
number in the hundreds, Tanya is circulating a survey to all stakeholders
asking their opinions on how unknown VO's should be treated in the BIRT
reporting tool (and if the BIRT tool should continue to be maintained/supported at all).

Steve Timm
