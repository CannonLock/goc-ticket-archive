Hi Helene

I suggest to proceed as follows

- The VO needs to be registered in the portal, in order to make the VO ID card accessible to site administrators in EGI

- this is based on the assumption that the VO manager of the existing VO at OSG will accept other non US users, but I assume this is not an issue.
