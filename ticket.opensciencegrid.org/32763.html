    Feb 27, 2017 09:03 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi Lincoln,

Those jobs were running from at least three distinct sites (iu.edu, campuscluster.illinois.edu and
mwt2.org), the problem was basically too many concurrent connections.  The DPM is not equipped to handle more than 200 concurrent connections to the SRM door.  Usually this is not a problem, as the SRM interface is used to do administrative commands, like "give me a gsiftp TURL to the file" or "tell me if the file is there" or "delete the file" so they complete quickly.  I can think of a couple reasons why the XENON jobs give problems.

1) the jobs are using the SRM door not just to get a TURL (eg gsiftp) to the file, but are actually using the SRM interface to copy the file, hence the commands take a long time to complete?

2) all of the data is on the Nikhef SE so all of the jobs are speaking to the same SE?  This is different than eg ATLAS that has several hundred SEs scattered all across the grid.
