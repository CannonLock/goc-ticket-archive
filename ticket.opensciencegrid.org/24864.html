    oasis finished its update without intervention by me. It (and oasis-login) went green light shortly after that.

oasis-replica apparently crashed during a snapshot. When restarted it did not clean up tis lock files
preventing any other snapshots. cvmfs_server processes accumulated (1 every 15 minutes) after that.

1) I rebooted oasis-replica (killing all the zombie processes)
2) Manually removed the lock files:
# find /srv/cvmfs/*.*/data/txn -name "*.*" -mtime +2 2>/dev/null|xargs rm -f
# rmdir /var/spool/cvmfs/oasis.opensciencegrid.org/is_snapshotting
3) Manually snapshotted oasis.opensciencegrid.org

Went green light shortly after these.

Dave: you might want to update
https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/CvmFS/StratumOnes#Cleaning_out_temporary_files
with the rmdir command above.
