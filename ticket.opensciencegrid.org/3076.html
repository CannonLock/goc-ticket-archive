    Feb 8, 2007 09:11 PM UTC by Rob Quick 
    Sent this to the OSG Support Centers:

Support Centers,

The OSG GROW VO is requesting more sites support the it. Please see the following
statement from Eric Shook with an explanation of why the GROW VOs is looking for
addition support:

"The GROW team is currently developing a generic submission tool for our VO users. We
are currently writing a conference paper demonstrating the capabilities of this tool
and describing its architecture. We plan to include an OSG use case in this paper.
The use case will include performance benchmarks (i.e. throughput) on OSG. To achieve
maximum throughput we would like to have more sites supporting the GROW VO during
this experiment. Any support would be greatly appreciated by the GROW team and VO
members."

To provide support to the GROW VO please make sure sites have the latest VO package.
Details for this package and instruction for updating can be found at
http://www.grid.iu.edu/news/news.php?id=59. Sites will also need to create the local
'grow' account.

The GOC is asking that all OSG Support Centers take an active roll in helping us get
the GROW VO activated on as many sites as possible.

Thank You,
Rob Quick
