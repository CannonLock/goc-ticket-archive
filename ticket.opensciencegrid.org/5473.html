    Aug 27, 2008 06:02 PM UTC by echism 
    To be clear--it looks like two things are happening:

1) in many, but not all, new submissions, the initial globus-job-manager
process is hanging around much longer than it should, even though
the condor job has successfully started and it could and should exit.

2) in cases where the malformed job (which most of them are) is killed,
the users' client is then attempting to do a restart by contacting
fermigridosg1.fnal.gov/jobmanager instead of fermigridosg1.fnal.gov/jobmanager-condor

This doesn't and can't work.

We really need to get a handle on this non-standard nimrod client
and the user's application before he continues wasting cycles
across the OSG.  In particular we need to be sure that
error conditions when the site cancels  a job are handled correctly and it looks very
much that this is not the case.

Steve Timm
