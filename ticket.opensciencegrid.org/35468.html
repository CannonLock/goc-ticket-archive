    Hi Aaron,

The problem with /cvmfs/cms.cern.ch is probably independent of the problem with /cvmfs/cms.hep.wisc.edu.

I do not know why the cms.cern.ch repository is not working on e318.  The problem you found with cms.hep.wisc.edu is indeed due to outdated configuration.  cvmfs03 does not exist anymore.  cvmfs02.hep.wisc.edu should be used instead.  We achieve that in /etc/cvmfs/config.d/cms.hep.wisc.edu.conf with the following:

CVMFS_SERVER_URL="http://cvmfs01.hep.wisc.edu/cvmfs/cms.hep.wisc.edu;http://cvmfs02.hep.wisc.edu/cvmfs/cms.hep.wisc.edu";
CVMFS_PUBLIC_KEY=/etc/cvmfs/keys/cms.hep.wisc.edu.pub

While we are discussing cvmfs configuration, could you check that /etc/cvmfs/config.d/cms.cern.ch.local contains the following?

export CMS_LOCAL_SITE=T2_US_Wisconsin

In the old days, that setting was different.  It pointed to /cvmfs/cms.hep.wisc.edu/osg/app/cmssoft/SITECONF/T2_US_Wisconsin instead.  With the above configuration, CMS jobs no longer need /cvmfs/cms.hep.wisc.edu.  They can rely solely on /cvmfs/cms.cern.ch.
