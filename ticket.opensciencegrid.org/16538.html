    Aug 19, 2013 07:38 PM UTC by Suchandra Thapa 
    Hi,

There's a bug in osg-configure when you use variable substitution across files (e.g. variable defined in 10-foo.ini and then used in 20-condor.ini).  I have a patch in testing but until the new rpms are released, you can fix this by either replacing the variable substitution with a hard-coded value or define the variable that you're substituting at the beginning of the section.
