    This should be resolved. The issue was that two fold.

One, the lesser issue, was that Caltech needed to look at why their fallback was failing.
The second, the greater issue was related to;

- Openscience grid no longer deploys lcg-tools in their distribution
- UCSD deployed new nodes that grabbed the latest OSG version, without lcg-tools
- Stageout at UCSD was configured to use lcg-tools
- lcg-tools absence on nodes caused a stageout error

The quick solution was I installed lcg-tools manually on those new systems. The long term solution will be to update the configuration at UCSD to use GFAL and not LCG tools.

In any case I believe this ticket should be closed.
