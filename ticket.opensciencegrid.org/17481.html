    After a little discussion with the person who wrote the privacy policy that was required by the university, it should be noted that on this page: https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/IUPrivacyPolicy

=================
We may make some personally-identifiable information available, such as:

your name
your email address
other contact information, such as address or telephone number

These are fairly clear-cut terms and they illustrate exactly what happened.  We have tried to limit access to these things as much as possible, but unfortunately with certificate requests they can't be avoided at the current time, based on how the approval procedure works.
