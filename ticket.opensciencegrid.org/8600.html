You may also want to check on your local collector in MySql to see if
maybe there is a problem reporting to it.  This is the query I used.

SELECT s.SiteName
,p.ProbeName
,max(EndTime)
from
Site s,
Probe p,
JobUsageRecord_Meta jm,
JobUsageRecord j
where
s.SiteName = 'UCSDT2'
and s.siteid = p.siteid
and p.probename like 'condor%'
and p.ProbeName  = jm.ProbeName
and jm.ServerDate > '2010-05-14'

    and jm.dbid = j.dbid
group by s.SiteName, p.ProbeName
;

The 'like' in the where clause was just to eliminate a bunch of gridftp
probes I saw in Gratia.
