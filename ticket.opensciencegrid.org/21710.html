    Hi,
Looks like fetch-crl doesn't like the fact that the cert and crl-url filenames don't match. I was able to eliminate the errors by doing:
# cp DigiCertGridTESTRootCA.cer DigiCertGridTEST-Root.pem
# cp DigiCertGridTESTCA-1.cer DigiCertGridTEST-Classic.pem

Not sure if the cer vs pem extension is an issue or not, but figured I should be consistent.
