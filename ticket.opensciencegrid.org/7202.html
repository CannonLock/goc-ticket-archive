To all,

Now that I understand that the date/time of failure is in UTC time,
the problem is much simpler and clearer.  We occasionally have a
problem with this volume in AFS where, during a backup volume
creation on the volume the volume goes offline.  This is, I believe,
a problem with AFS version 1.4.10.  The problem occurred this
morning at 01:02 ET (05:02 UTC) when the backup volume was
created.  Since we know this occasionally occurs, I have a script
in place that detects this and brings the volume back online.  This
happened at 01:10 ET (05:10 UTC).  Since the job failures occurred
shortly after this I am attributing this to a delay between when the
problem is fixed and when the server actually makes the volume
available to clients although I would have thought that would be
seconds rather than minutes.  It was also my understanding that
this volume was no longer used in production.  This has been
fixed as well.  I will verify this tomorrow.

We will be moving to AFS version 1.4.11 which supposedly fixes this
problem.  This was released yesterday and we were anxiously
awaiting it.  The original schedule to deploy was July 28th. to
coincide with other maintenance but this may be moved up.

Regards,
John M.
