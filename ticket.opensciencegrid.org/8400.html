    Thanks, I took a look at it.

Yeah, Pacman is really picky when you move to a new platform, and it's hard to overcome. If you installed on ROCKS 4, it doesn't want you to do updates on ROCKS 5 because in general binaries are not compatible and you're doomed to weird errors.

In your case you got your installation to keep working by installing the older version of OpenSSL, so you really could keep running the old version. You were lucky--in general this may not work.

I don't think there is any safe way of getting Pacman to do an update in this case. Sorry about that! I think the only thing you can really do is a fresh installation.

If you don't have time to do that for a while, you can probably get the VO update by hand. It's messy, but I think you can do it if you want to. Does that interest you?
