    The last time something like this happened was about two years ago. In that case Java upgraded from 1.70.25 to 1.70.45 and the authentication now required 1024 bits. At the time the default for grid-proxy-int, which rsv used, was 512 bits. (See https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/17802) Since openssl just updated, I thought that this was a similar problem. While I was trying to sort out that problem I received a call from the GOC about an unrelated matter (when I would complete the migration from gram to htcondor on my CE.) He kindly initiated this ticket. I tried restarting globus-gridftp-server and bestman2 on my SE, but that did not help. Late in the day I restarted frontier-squid, our proxy server and that seemed to fix the problem, at least for RSV. I wanted to wait a day or so to make sure before responding to the ticket. Since RSV has been working I think you may be able to close this ticket. Sorry for all the trouble.
