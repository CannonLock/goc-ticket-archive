    Nov 3, 2014 04:32 PM UTC 
    Sorry for delay, but late Friday I think a resolution was found with help of Steve Johnson and Burt Holzman.

Change
queue_info_cmd = 'sinfo -h -o "%P %a %C %l"'
to
queue_info_cmd = 'sinfo -h -o "%R %a %C %l"'

That is in /usr/lib/python2.6/site-packages/slurm_common.py around line #21

The %P option outputs a default partition as "serial*" which is not how the jobs in squeue are printed.  This likely caused issues when jobs from that partition were output from squeue and sinfo had no matching partition name.

- Trey
