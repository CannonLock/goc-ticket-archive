Data Center community,

On Monday, March 12, from 5 to 7am, we will be performing maintenance on the Data Center routers at IU Bloomington and IUPUI. During this period, we will upgrade one router at each campus to newer code. There may be a brief service interruption while traffic moves to the secondary router and back after the upgrade.

On Wednesday March 14, we will upgrade the second Data Center routers at IU Bloomington and IUPUI. The secondary maintenance on Wednesday morning should have no customer impact, as the routers being upgraded are backups.

Additionally, we will be making a change to the way traffic is routed between the CORE security zones at each campus, effectively merging the  zones. This means that firewall rules will no longer be necessary for hosts within the CORE security zone to communicate across campus with each other. Please see the link for a PDF with details on this change, including a VLAN listing for each zone.

We have presented these changes at ESIT and Change Management. Please contact me if you have any questions or concerns.
