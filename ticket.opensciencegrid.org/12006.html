On 04/20/2012 12:44 PM, Open Science Grid FootPrints wrote:

> cdf

That's your problem: you don't have a "cdf" account. (Or a bunch of other accounts that you probably want as well.)

The user-vo-map is a file that lists all the VOs you support and the accounts that they are mapped to. There are two things required for the VO to be supported when you use GUMS:

1) You have to authorize the VO in GUMS.
2) You have to have the account on your system for the VO.

osg-configure will only let you list a group as a sponsor if it's in the user-vo-map. Since you don't the account, it's not listed in the user-vo-map, and osg-configure complains.

Your solution is to make the accounts you need to support the VOs you wish to support.
