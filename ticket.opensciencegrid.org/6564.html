    Mar 17, 2009 08:03 PM UTC by Jeff Porter 
    Hi Rob & Kyle,

Thanks for jumping in on this.  It seems to me that there are three voms
items,

1) adding roles in the gums.template:  These additions are based on my
understanding of GUMS and the gums.template.  I'll contact GUMS admins
(Jay) directly for confirmation.  Specifically, I read that:

'name' in groupMapping is GUMS specific Group-to-User mapping name.
'name' in userGroup is GUMS specific User details name
'voXXX' are VOMS specific identifiers
'groupName' in accountMapping is the unix account name

Hence the three groupMappings are:

<groupMapping name='alice' accountingVo='alice'
accountingDesc='ALICE'>
<userGroup className='gov.bnl.gums.VOMSGroup'
url='https://voms.cern.ch:8443/voms/alice/services/VOMSAdmin';
persistenceFactory='mysql'
name='alice'
voGroup="/alice"
sslCertfile='/etc/grid-security/http/httpcert.pem'
sslKey='/etc/grid-security/http/httpkey.pem'
matchFQAN="vo" acceptProxyWithoutFQAN='true' />
<accountMapping className='gov.bnl.gums.GroupAccountMapper'
groupName='alice' />
</groupMapping>

<groupMapping name='aliceLCGAdmin' accountingVo='alice'
accountingDesc='ALICE'>
<userGroup className='gov.bnl.gums.VOMSGroup'
url='https://voms.cern.ch:8443/voms/alice/services/VOMSAdmin';
persistenceFactory='mysql'

    name='aliceLCGAdmin'
voGroup="/alice"
voRole="lcgadmin"
sslCertfile='/etc/grid-security/http/httpcert.pem'
sslKey='/etc/grid-security/http/httpkey.pem'
matchFQAN="vo" acceptProxyWithoutFQAN='true' />
<accountMapping className='gov.bnl.gums.GroupAccountMapper'
groupName='alicelcgadmin' />
</groupMapping>

<groupMapping name='alicePilot' accountingVo='alice'
accountingDesc='ALICE'>
<userGroup className='gov.bnl.gums.VOMSGroup'
url='https://voms.cern.ch:8443/voms/alice/services/VOMSAdmin';
persistenceFactory='mysql'
name='alicePilot'
voGroup="/alice"
voRole="pilot"
sslCertfile='/etc/grid-security/http/httpcert.pem'
sslKey='/etc/grid-security/http/httpkey.pem'
matchFQAN="vo" acceptProxyWithoutFQAN='true' />
<accountMapping className='gov.bnl.gums.GroupAccountMapper'
groupName='alicepilot' />
</groupMapping>
