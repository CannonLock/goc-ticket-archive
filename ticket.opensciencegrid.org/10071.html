    One more update--with Faarooq's latest personal cert that had gridadmin
privileges, we had him request a new host cert using gridadmin and
the SSL_server purpose is there in that certificate.  So the
main issue all along was the fact that the earlier cert we generated
did not have an SSL_Server purpose field in it.  I did not
understand the difference between SSL_Server showing in the x509
certificate extensions, and SSL_server being an authorized purpose for
the cert.

That still leaves the mystery of why some of our services worked just
fine and are still working just fine with a cert with no SSL_Server field
in it. And also not clear what really changed in the process of faarooq's
five personal certs such that cert-gridadmin works now.  But we are
set and can make working and well-formed certificates that work for
all known applications now so I think now the remaining mysteries are just
a curiosity.
