    Hi Ian,
thank you very much for your feedback on the troubleshooting instructions.

As I thought, the problem is in the configuration of GIP: you are
missing a critical attribute: GlueCEUniqueID.

I've seen your questions about configure-gip: I'll forward them to the
gip group. I do not understand why they are different.

About your other questions/comments:
- The difference in path (gip/ vs. lcg/) depends on the OSG version.
gip/ is for vdt v1.10.1 i.e. OSG v1.0: I say it at the beginning, but
probably it isn't conspicuous enough: I should probably keep both
versions...

- GlueCEName: in our implementation of the Glue Schema model, for
condor, we have a CE per VO and this is where the name is coming from;
for PBS, it is the name of the local batch system queue.

- "I see from here:
https://osg-ress-1.fnal.gov:8443/ReSS/ReSS-prd-History.html that ReSS is
OK. Isn't this linked to CEMon?"
ReSS is logically composed of 2 pieces: CEMon at the sites and the ReSS
central services at osg-ress-1 (condor_collector & co.). When CEMon
starts up, it registers site info to the central services. If your site
works properly, you should see it at the link above: we take a sample
every 6 hours; however, you can check any time (...without waiting for 6
hours...) with the condor_status command that you have done. If there is
no entry, your site does not register.

Cheers
Gabriele
