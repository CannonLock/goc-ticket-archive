Elizabeth is right--this is an unsupported platform. You can look at
the VDT's documentation on attempting to install on a supported
platform:
http://vdt.cs.wisc.edu/releases/1.10.1/installation_advanced.html#unsup_plat

You can also see our list of supported platforms:
http://vdt.cs.wisc.edu/releases/1.10.1/requirements.html

I'm sorry we don't support Fedora, but it's such a quickly moving
target that it's hard for us to keep up with. We tend to support
platforms that have longer support lifetimes.
