    Could you post the default_remote_cerequirements that you're using? Is '#PBS -A "account"' really not a valid invocation of Torque's "-A"? What version of torque are you using? if [ -n "$AccountingGroup" ]; then
echo "#PBS -A $AccountingGroup" | tr -d '"'
fi
