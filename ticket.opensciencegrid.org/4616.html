    Hey Steven,

Look at the following link:

http://is.grid.iu.edu/cgi-bin/show_source_data?which=NYSGRID_CORNELL_NYS1&source=cemon

The problem is with the entry that starts out with:

dn:
GlueLocationLocalID
=
SAMPLE_SCRATCH
,GlueSubClusterUniqueID
=nys1.cac.cornell.edu,GlueClusterUniqueID=nys1.cac.cornell.edu,mds-vo-
name=local,o=grid

The entry for:

GlueLocationVersion:

is left blank for your site.  You probably don't even want to
advertise this software (as it is just the default "fake" entry).

You can fix this problem by removing the line starting with
"SAMPLE_SCRATCH" from $OSG_APP/grid3-locations.txt
