    Oct 23, 2006 06:22 PM UTC by Horst Severini 
    Hi Rob and Steve,

as far as I know, it's simply not possible to run Standard Universe jobs
at all in the grid environment, be it via globus-submission or Condor-G,
or any other way that involves the globus gatekeeper/jobmanager-condor.

The only way you can get that to work is by submitting to the local
(and by local I mean BNL) Condor pool directly, or possibly via
Condor-Glidein, but I don't know anything about that.

At least that's what I gathered from talking with Miron Livny the other
day.

Hope this helps,

Horst
