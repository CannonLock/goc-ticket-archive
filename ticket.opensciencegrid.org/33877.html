Hi Bockjoo,

it should be simple as the other routes you already define. Is it mixture of nodes you want to run singularity on or can you for example separate them to
distinguish by target like +distro="RHEL7" for instance? That would look like in your JOB_ROUTE:

requirements = TARGET.distro=?="RHEL7";\

As refresher here more recipes to look at:
https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Documentation/Release3/JobRouterRecipes

Singularity jobs must have +WantSingularity=True attribute in the job submit, I believe that's what CMS Frontend guys were testing already. On the
Factory side we already have set of entries using '+WantSingularity="True"' as expression in the entry for these jobs. In addition, the glidein will have
then on our side characteristics of GLIDEIN_Singularity_Use=True in the entry.

Consider what would be best for you according to ROUTE entry requirement and we can change in the factory config to match that attribute and also set
+WantSingularity=True ClassAd which will result in your submissions being routed to a group of machines that are running RHEL7 (if you take that
example) that have the singularity RPMs installed.

Let us know how you want to proceed, for the record I'm adding factory support list, we've corresponding ticket you opened in GGUS:
https://ggus.eu/index.php?mode=ticket_info&;ticket_id=128386.

Hope that helps!

Thanks,
Marian
