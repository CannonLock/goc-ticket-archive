    Mar 3, 2017 06:36 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Our investigation indicates that the error seems to occur when the destination site requires a checksum. We don't store any checksums on our storage system, so then the transfer fails. Have several sites recently enabled use of checksums? We haven't stored checksums for a long time, so we are wondering why the problem has just appeared this week.

Please advise us about checksums and FTS3. Have there been recent changes? We don't store checksums on our storage system because we found they could not be easily managed and tended to accumulate without control. Are we required now to enable checksums? It will take a significant amount of time to obtain checksums for all of our files.
