    Nov 4, 2016 06:49 PM UTC by Dave Dykstra 
    The last time I saw this error message, someone had recently upgraded to cvmfs-2.3.2 and they ran into a problem because CVMFS_HTTP_PROXY did not have an "http://" in it.  Is that the case for you?  There's a CVMFS ticket about that change in 2.3.X https://sft.its.cern.ch/jira/browse/CVM-1045.  You're likely failing all accesses to new data, but the user experiences only some errors because the rest of the data is in the worker node cache.
