Neha,

Actually I just did what I wrote in my original email
and that seemed to have worked. Dmitry's was
a good way to preserve the old (but possibly corrupt)
database....whereas mine just overwrote it.

On the Friday, I heard back from others who use dCache
and they told me the way I was going to do it was fine...

So I did...

pg_resetxlog

(now startup postgresql as root)

pg_dumpall > dumpfile
psql -f dumpfile postgres

I was able to get back up Friday evening and so far
all seems to be well....
