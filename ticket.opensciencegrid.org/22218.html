    Hi Tanya,

Unfortunately all we have are workarounds for now.

The two ways around this currently are

(1) comment out the pthread line in /etc/gridftp.d/gridftp-hdfs.conf:

# $GLOBUS_THREAD_MODEL pthread

or (2), run with parallelism > 1:

globus-url-copy -p 2 gsiftp://$server//hadoop/osg/irods/home/goc/nt.5-30-2014/nt.11.tar.gz file:///${PWD}/a1
