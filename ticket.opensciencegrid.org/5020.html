    May 23, 2008 04:16 PM UTC by Neha Sharma 
    A warning about FTS/dCache transfers...there is an FTS timeout settable
via the -m flag.  For our site it was 120 seconds(I think this is the
default).  FTS expects to see a "transfer marker" coming back from the
destination within that timeout.  The default for dcache (in
gridftpdoor.batch) is to set to 180 seconds!   For our site this meant
large files frequently got TRANSFER_TIMEOUT and were killed.

Check the setting you have in .../d-cache/config/gridftpdoor.batch for
the performanceMarkerPeriod.  Ours is:

"set context -c performanceMarkerPeriod 30"  (I changed from 180
originally)

Shawn
