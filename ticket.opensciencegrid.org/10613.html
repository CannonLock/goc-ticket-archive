-- by Michael Moore
    Jun 25, 2011 04:18 PM UTC 
    Hi Derek.

I don't think it is a good idea selectively removing wget from the OSG WN package;
if wget is broken, most of the rest of the OSG WN client is likely broken as well.

As for
regexp(".*CentOS_5.*", CondorPlatform)
there are 2 pitfalls:
1) This tests what version of condor is installed, not what platform is the WN running.
(one could reasonably install a SL5 condor on a SL6 node, right?)
2) This will likely skyrocket the negotiation times... regexp is typically quite expensive.

Igor
