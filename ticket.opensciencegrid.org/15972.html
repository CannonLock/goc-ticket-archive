Could this ticket please be re-assigned to SLAC?

The problem is that SLAC is not publishing the SE in the BDII. Because the surls are not full (the port is missing), FTS3 needs to query the BDII to find out what port to use. The only reason why FTS2 transfers are working is that the SLAC SE was at some point in the BDII and hence is in the BDII cache.
