    Peter,

Chris from gratia sent this update earlier and would like your comments if you have any:

The "Reporting Name" is a label assigned by the VO admin (not individual users) and describes how jobs run by that VO should be viewed in Gratia reports. The convention for the default name is that it should be the same as the top-level VO name, although there are notable exceptions such as, "usatlas" being the reporting name for all ATLAS jobs on the OSG.

When last I checked, "/NEBioGrid" was the reporting name for your VO, which has caused comment in various areas of the OSG. Removal of the / in the reporting name on OIM (with *no* change required in any VO-internal groups or roles) will bring your reporting name into compliance with the convention used by other VOs on the OSG. As I said, this will have *no* effect on anything your users do, including interaction with VOMS or VOMRS or with sites.

Concerns over whether non-latini-1 characters in your users' DNs can be handled technically by the Gratia system are a different question and a GOC ticket should be entered if a particular incident should arise.

Thanks for your help,

Chris Green for Gratia-Ops.
