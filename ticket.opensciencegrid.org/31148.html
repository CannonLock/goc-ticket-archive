    Sep 6, 2016 12:12 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    The FermiGrid sites FNAL_GPGRID_4 and FNAL_GPGRID_5 make use of the glideinWMS feature Glidein_Site_Collector.
This is used to advertise the glidein classad to the local (port 9619) collector on the CE.
Recently on August 17 this feature stopped working on these two sites, although it is still working on the CE's of the CMS tier 1 which
are very similarly configured.  The only difference we have been able to find to date, is that the GOC/SDSC factories appear to
be sending a newer condor tarball of version 8.4.8, whereas the factories of the CMS global pool are sending condor 8.4.2.

The goal of this ticket is to figure out if a change was made to the GOC/SDSC factories around that time to up the condor version.
The symptom we are seeing is that the glidein no longer trusts the cert of the gatekeeper, even though STARTD.GSI_DAEMON_NAME is
set to blank.  We are contacting HTCondor staff  in a separate ticket to see if anything in the change between 8.4.2 and 8.4.8 could account for this.
