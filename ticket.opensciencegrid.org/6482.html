    Jeffrey,

We need a few things to complete your registration. They are as follows:

- a site name: this is typically the name of the cluster where your resource is
installed, but any name is okay.
- a facility name: the name of your institution or organization.
- what support center, if any, you intend to support your resource (ie LIGO, USCMS,
USATLAS)
- your longitude and latitude so we can display your site on our map.
- one successful run of site_verify.pl to verify that your resource is passing
critical tests
- Please attend our next weekly operation call (see
http://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Operations/ProductionMeetingMinutes) to answer any
questions the community may have.

Thank you
