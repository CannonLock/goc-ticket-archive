    Dec 14, 2009 06:27 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    This is really a request for a change in policy but I will put it
as a feature request for OIM because that is the closest thing in the menu.
On a number of occasions we have seen either VO's or Resources
be registered to the OSG listing "Fermilab" as their support center,
but they did not talk to us at all to ask us first and they are well outside the
defined scope of our Support Center.  the latest is
the uva_cms_se which is trying to do it currently.

I would like to request that it be added to the registration workflow
in OIM that whenever some VO or Resource or Resource Group tries
to register and list a Support Center, that E-mail be sent out
to the contacts listed for that Support Center, making sure that
the Support Center approves this request.  I follow the operations
call regularly and look at the registrations in advance, but a
recent audit of the database that I performed, showed me
even more out there that I didn't previously know about were claiming
us as a support center.  This has been a problem since the
beginning of the OSG and is now getting frequent enough that it needs
to be addressed.

Steve Timm
