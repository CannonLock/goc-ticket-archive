    May 27, 2010 02:15 PM UTC by Steven Clark 
    The simple probe jobs running on the GLOW site are simple shell scripts and do
not make use of signals.  Perhaps the signal 10 is the result of an eviction
request?

Jobs that are caught in the evicted state are terminated via a condor_rm.  By
caught I mean the job has a particular status when condor does its periodic
status check.  Our experience with evicted jobs is that they will be repeatedly
evicted and a run to completion is highly unlikely.  In such situations we are
better off sending the job to another site.
