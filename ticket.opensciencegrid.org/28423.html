    Feb 29, 2016 06:17 PM UTC by bonnland@.... 
    Hi Carl,

Yes, Eric Nienhouse and I talked with Brian Bockelm on Thursday.   It was a very good discussion.

We quickly determined that what NCAR needs is a version of Bestman that can provide filesystem access to a physical tape archive that itself lacks a POSIX-compliant filesystem interface.   This capability is offered in what used to be called the "full mode" version of Bestman.    If I understood Brian correctly, there is a chance that OSG Bestman will work in "full mode",  but this is not a common use case, it has not been tested lately, and it's possible that certain portions of the original full-mode capability may have been inadvertently removed.  

We also talked about other software solutions that various labs are pursuing for providing file access to HPSS tape archives. We believe Brian's information was valuable, and it most likely will help us come up with a workable plan as new hardware and systems are ordered and come online in the future. 

Many thanks for your help!

--Brian Bonnlander
