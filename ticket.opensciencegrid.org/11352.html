    Dec 19, 2011 02:19 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi,

As of Friday, we tuned the CERN top-level BDII with help of Laurence.  The increase of the timeout had caused problems with the overall service (the tree was not being updated) and was causing the BDII to serve incomplete results.

The following actions were taken on Friday:

- The timeout was reset back to the default 30 seconds.

- Caching was turned on -> the top-level BDII should now serve a stale record in case the real record cannot be obtained from the site (for up to 1 day).

- A problem with a remote site which had been overloading the CERN firewall (noted in the WLCG operatons meeting on Friday) was resolved. The times of this problem are co-incident with the degradation of the CERN BDII service wrt. the visibility of the OSG sites. We believe this was the root cause of the problem.

cheers,
Gavin
