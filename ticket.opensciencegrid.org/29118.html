    Apr 12, 2016 10:08 PM UTC by Brian Lin 
    Are you still having issues with your pool schedd on the CE where jobs look like they're coming from top.ucr.edu? If so, you should edit your condor config (/etc/condor/config.d) and set `NETWORK_HOSTNAME = top.hep.int` and `NETWORK_INTERFACE = <IP of top.hep.int>`. Once you do that, you should set `JOB_ROUTER_SCHEDD2_NAME = top.hep.int` (or re-run `osg-configure -c`).

That issue is different than the one that we're seeing in your CE SchedLog, which is caused by the fact that you've set `UID_DOMAIN = hep.int`. It should be set to 'users.opensciencegrid.org'. Once you change that, the SAM tests (and my test jobs) should at least land on your CE. If you need the UID_DOMAIN of jobs submitted to your pool to be hep.int, these changes should be made in /etc/condor/config.d. In general, any configuration you need for jobs to be submitted to your pool should be made in /etc/condor/config.d.
