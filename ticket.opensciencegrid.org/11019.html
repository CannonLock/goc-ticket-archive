    Hi John,

The WLCG MOU requires that the sites publish their installed capacity to the WLCG management board, and through them to the RRB etc.  So that's who uses it.  We write this information by hand into OIM because we want to report our MOU capacity, not our actual capacity, as what we have on the floor beyond what we pledge is none of their business.  I presume the comparison is to make sure we are in sync, although I personally haven't been involved in that piece of it.  I don't know the story on the ALICE VO; I'm not sure what, if anything, we've pledged to them.  You'd have to ask someone from ALICE.  What SPRACE does is really up to SPRACE management, although I for sure am not inclined to be responsible for whatever numbers are reported for them.  Hope this helps, best wishes.

Ken
