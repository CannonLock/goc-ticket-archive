    An announcement of the VDT 1.6.1 package went out today. Closing this ticket.
    Mar 20, 2007 08:06 PM UTC by VDT 
    This problem was solved in VDT 1.6.1c, which was released about an hour
ago. If you update the Configure-GIP package, you'll get a new
configure_gip that pays attention to the GLOBUS_HOSTNAME environment
variable.

Note that we took the easy way out here: only configure_gip pays
attention to this variable, and not the entire VDT. It's arguable that
the entire VDT should do so, but we decided to get out a quick fix in
favor of a more comprehensive, but slower fix.

Details are available online at:
http://vdt.cs.wisc.edu/releases/1.6.1/release.html

I'm going to resolve this ticket, but feel free to re-open it if you
have any concerns at all.
