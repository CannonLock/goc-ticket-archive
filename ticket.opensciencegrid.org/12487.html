    Aug 13, 2012 12:05 PM UTC by Scott Teige 
    Hello,

OSG-GridFtp became OSG-GridFTP, I believe at 3.1.6. As part of passing the results to WLCG we transform OSG-GridFtp to
GridFtp and send it along. There was no rule for OSG-GridFTP so the records were sent untransformed and were rejected
on the receiving end. This caused WLCG to show some sites as "Unknown".

We have installed a rule for OSG-GridFTP and resent the data from 10/Jul up to installation of the new rule. This has corrected
the data at the WLCG end and prevents incorrect data from moving on.

Bottom line, this is not an emergency but fixing it would be good.

S
