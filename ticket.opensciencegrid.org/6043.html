    Nov 20, 2008 08:26 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi Jon/Steve,

We've not received RSV records from NYGRID-CCR-U2 since yesterday afternoon.

I dug into it, and found out the following:

-- First, your new Apache config is not configured to serve RSV pages -- i.e I can't even tell if RSV is running or if it's not. I don't know how you landed up with a vdt-webapps.conf file that does not have the RSV bits but I know how you can get those bits in it :) ..

-- but wait ... The problem is even more basic; I think you got an upgrade to osg-rsv (and some other packages like Apache) but you do not seem to have re-run its configuration steps. I know you have a complex RSV setup ..
-- so the easiest thing to do might be to grab configure_osg_rsv execution lines from your notes/Unix history, and re-run them
-- Or, you could copy the submission files from a backup

vdt-control --off apache
vdt-control --off osg-rsv
cp -pr $BACKUP_LOCATION/osg-rsv/submissions/probes/* $VDT_LOCATION/osg-rsv/submissions/probes/
cp -pr $BACKUP_LOCATION/osg-rsv/submissions/consumers/* $VDT_LOCATION/osg-rsv/submissions/consumers/

$VDT_LOCATION/osg-rsv/setup/configure_osg_rsv --setup-for-apache
vdt-control --on apache
vdt-control --on osg-rsv

[For the record, we're looking into making this process much simpler, and based on a config file in next version of RSV]

Let me know how you'd like to proceed, and I'll be happy to assist further.

Cheers,
Arvind
