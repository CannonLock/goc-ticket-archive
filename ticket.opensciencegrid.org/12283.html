There were 52,546 tests performed.

This report uses the WLCG availability algorithm to determine the availability based upon RSV test results.

The algorithm is documented here:

https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/pub/Operations/RSVPeriodicReporting/Gridview_Service_Availability_Computation-1.pdf

The weekly and monthly averages are done by applying the above algorithm to a longer time interval; they are not
necessarily the average of the daily availability scores.

The table shows the daily, weekly, monthly, and the month to date (MTD) reliability and availability.  The "monthly" calculation includes the last thirty days, while the MTD is from the start of the month through the first day of the report.  Weekly is 7 days not including the previous day.
