    Hi Allan,

It's my understanding that Condor glideins require worker nodes to have
a 2-way communication channel.  Normally, this is a direct network connection,
but our cluster's worker nodes do not have Internet connectivity.

There is a thing called Generic Connection Brokering (GCB) that lets
one get around these restrictions.

http://www.cs.wisc.edu/condor/manual/v7.2/3_7Networking_includes.html#sec:;GCB

I'm not sure if it will help in your situation (I'm no Condor expert!)
but it might.

Brian
