    Can you try some of these transfers again? I think we've narrowed this issue down to a bug in gridftp-hdfs-0.5.4-16.osg32.el6.x86_64.rpm that was released back in April. Downgrading all our servers to the prior gridftp-hdfs-0.5.4-11.osg32.el6.x86_64.rpm seems to have eliminated this issue.
