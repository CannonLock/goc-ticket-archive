The closure email was made to be short and simple, only including ticket
number and title.
The idea is that if the ticket needs to be re-opened or the issue is not
resolved, the GOC
needs to be notified of this so they can re-open the ticket.

More detailed information is normally received from ticket update emails
(current fields, summary, etc).

As for the web-link, it is no longer used due to tightening the security of
the ticketing system.
Also, even if a web-link were included, you would still have to have a
Footprints account to login to view tickets as we do not allow open
customer viewing of tickets.  The only other way to get ticket info is via
assignee/customer ticket update emails, from the GOC, or a brief review in
the weekly trouble ticket summaries (posted on the GOC ops pages).

I hope this answers your questions.  Anything further from a more
procedural standpoint can be clarified by Tim.

regards,

    Tom
