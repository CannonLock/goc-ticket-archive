    Feb 11, 2009 08:39 PM UTC by Horst Severini 
    Hi Charles,

I just poked around turner a bit, and I think it's possible that this
soft link is causing problems:

/etc/grid-security/certificates -> /etc/grid-security/certificates-35-1

since that's pointing to a quite old certificates area, and depending
on the incoming client software, it may look there instead of the generic
$GLOBUS_LOCATION/TRUSTED_CA , which does point to the correct and
updated location,

/usr/local/osg-0.8.0/globus/share/certificates

(which is a soft link to certificates-46-1 ).

So I suggest you either remove that soft link /etc/grid-security/certificates,
or better yet, make it a new soft link to $GLOBUS_LOCATION/TRUSTED_CA ,
since some clients are stupid enough to REQUIRE
/etc/grid-security/certificates to exist, even though they shouldn't do that.

Hope this helps,

Horst
