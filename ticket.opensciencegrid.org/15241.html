    May 22, 2013 12:36 AM UTC by Carl Edquist 
    Hi Gang,

Yes, it looks like pacman does not support SLC6.  We have been phasing out pacman and support for it will end at the end of this month.  We recommend installing with RPMs instead, and the documentation for the latest release is here:

https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/bin/view/Documentation/Release3/

RHEL6/SL6 is supported, and SLC6 should be close enough to SL6 to work without any problems...

Does that work for you?

Thanks,
Carl
