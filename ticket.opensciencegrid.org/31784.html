    We tracked this down.  After the power outage we had a large datanode that decided to not mount half its disks.  This caused about 100TB to be missing in HDFS and cause massive amounts of replication.  We tracked down which host it was and recovered the mounts.  All files are available and replicated in HDFS now, so ther should not be any issues.  The files with the checksum errors are due to the cksum file not exiting at our site.  The actual files are ok in HDFS.
