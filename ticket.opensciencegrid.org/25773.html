    Hi Dorian,

I apologize for the delayed response. I've been out of town for a long weekend.

I've gone ahead and cleared out all the held glideins again. Like before, the hold reasons are quite varied across different VOs as are the errors in the logs of any failing glideins I've come across. As such, it's hard to pin down a specific problem for you to look into. Even more so since we don't get any logs back for held glideins, let alone specific worker nodes.

The one interesting observation I've noticed is that CMS glideins are almost always never held. For example, this might be easy to see if you compare the CERN factory statistics [1] to those of the GOC and SDSC factories [2] once the held glideins begin to build again. This leads me to believe that most of the issues are likely VO-specific and not necessarily Caltech site-specifc. That is, many of the other VOs may simply be experiencing high levels of preemption, which will throw their glideins into a held state. But some failing glidein logs I've come across also point to possible issues that some VO frontends have been exhibiting at other sites too.

Let's simply go ahead and close out this ticket. Caltech is performing quite well overall despite this consistent level of held glideins, which isn't really affecting CMS. I'll keep an eye the held glideins and check back now and then to see if things improve or if I can find something more specific for you to track down on your end.

Thanks again for your help.

Marty Kandes
