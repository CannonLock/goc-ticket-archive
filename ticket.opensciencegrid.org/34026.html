    Hey David,

I'll repeat in case you didn't see the last email. Those messages are messages that you get when one of the following is true:
1) your certificate isn't loaded in your browser or browser-helper keystore as applicable
2) the browser has asked to access your certificate and you have pressed deny

If it's 1, you should load the cert, if 2, just restart your browser and let it read the cert. If you've lost the original cert that you received last year, let me know.
