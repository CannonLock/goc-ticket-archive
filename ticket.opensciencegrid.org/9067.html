    Aug 18, 2010 06:40 PM UTC by Doug Olson 
    The long term infrastructure issue (additional CRL download locations) may be addressed in the future but not soon.
If your machine is still being blocked then you should contact the CA directly, srce-ca@...., and tell them
the hostname / ip address of the machine being blocked and see if they will unblock it.
It will also be useful if the SRCE network people will say why your node was blocked, perhaps it was
not due to CRL downloads but for some other reason.
It will be helpful if you cc this ticket email address in your question to SRCE so that
progress is tracked.
For future reference, the contact information for CAs are included in the .info files in
the trusted CA directory, eg.,
$ cat /etc/grid-security/certificates/ff94d436.info
alias = SRCE
url = http://ra.srce.hr
ca_url = http://ra.srce.hr/cacert.pem
crl_url = http://ra.srce.hr/cacrl.crl
email = srce-ca@....
status = accredited:classic
version = 1.36
sha1fp.0 = A0:39:C3:FD:D6:A3:5F:C5:94:5C:5B:87:89:0D:4C:32:8F:11:C7:E0
