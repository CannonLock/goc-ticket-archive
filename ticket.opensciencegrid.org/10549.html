This is indirectly related to your ticket #10460
DOEGrids signing cert - replacement "release candidate" ISSUE=10460 PROJ=71

The DOEGrids CA signing certificate used by various CERN production
services (supporting experiment collaboration sites typically) will
be changed  fairly soon.  I don't have a date - timing depends on some other
sequencing issues.  This new certificate has been  in test mode for
some weeks.

The change is to the CRL distribution points embedded in the certificate.
The risk is that errors or unavailability in these DP's will cause
DoS to DOEGrids certificate holders to CERN collaboration sites, like
we have seen several times in the past year.  This is outside OSG's scope
but it is likely that problem notices will show up at GOC.
