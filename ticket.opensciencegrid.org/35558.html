    Dec 8, 2017 08:08 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi Yutaro,  the latter is true, i.e. staging and file transfer are performed asynchronously. Stager agent checks which files are wanted based on routing and tries to stage them. Once the staging succeeds, files are exported by the Export agent at the source site, and only then the FTS transfer task is created and executed by Download agent at the destination site.

See descriptions on top of FileStager, FileExport, FileDownoad in https://github.com/dmwm/PHEDEX/blob/master/Toolkit/Transfer/ .
Cheers,
Natalia.
