    Jun 19, 2009 08:22 PM UTC by Parag Mhashilkar 
    Hi Wei,

ReSS is the resource selection service used by many OSG VOs. CEMon, is
the CE monitor service developed as part of glite. It is used to publish
information to the central ress services and to BDII.

When you configure your osg ce, the site should be automatically
configured to report its information to ReSS along with BDII. The
information you report to ress is the information reported by
osg-info-wrapper from GIP, but instead of LDAP, the info in ReSS is in
condor classad format. This enables many users to just use condor to do
the match making for their jobs. For detailed explanation you can refer
to - https://twiki.grid.iu.edu/twiki/bin/view/ResourceSelection/WebHome
