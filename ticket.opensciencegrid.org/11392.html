GOCTX Source: BNL Ticket: Queue=StorageManagement ID=21435
    Dec 20, 2011 10:47 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hi,

We have experienced general problems with file transfers between CERN and T2s. Could you please have a look at the services.xml file of your FTS server. We would need you to apply following changes:

1. Look for the wrong entry with <service name="httpg://srm-eosatlas.cern.ch:8443/srm/managerv2"> and remove it. (Please be careful not to remove a very similar one ending with "/srm/v2/server")

2. Add the entry
<service name="gsiftp://eosatlassftp.cern.ch:2811";>
<parameters>
<endpoint>gsiftp://eosatlassftp.cern.ch:2811/<;/endpoint>
<type>gsiftp</type>
<version>2.2.0</version>
<site>CERN-PROD</site>
<volist>
<vo>atlas</vo>
</volist>
</parameters>
</service>

Thanks in advance,
Fernando
