    Apr 17, 2017 09:49 PM UTC 
    Just a followup from our end.. We have consulted the HTCondor team because the ResidentSetSize of the glidein in question was reporting much bigger
than the sum of the ResidentSetSize of the individual processes within it.  One example--ResidentSetSize=75GB, ResidentSetSize_RAW=52GB,
but sum of ResidentSetSize of all processes was only 42GB.

They have suggested that we enable ProportionalSetSize on our cluster, which we will eventually do, but it may be a while. They have also informed us
that you can't use ResidentSetSize_RAW in a SYSTEM_PERIODIC_REMOVE expression because the condor_shadow doesn't know anything
about ResidentSetSize_RAW, it only knows ResidentSetSize and the rounding happens at the condor_schedd.

So we will eventually shift to using ProportionalSetSize in our cluster and when we do, then any hcc glideins that contain 8 jobs from user jeanjack will start
getting killed, as we had intended to do all along.   The jobs now coming from hcc are actually a mix of various users and our cluster is not in distress right now.
This ticket can be closed at this time, thanks to Derek for responding.

Steve Timm
