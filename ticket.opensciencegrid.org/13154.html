    Dec 10, 2012 09:39 PM UTC by Anand Padmanabhan 
    The security team noticed that the version of Condor being run at your site (7.8.1) is vulnerable to local privilege escalation and the attacker culd bypass the target site's host-based authentication. More details on the vulnerability can be found at http://research.cs.wisc.edu/htcondor/security/vulnerabilities/CONDOR-2012-0003.html and http://research.cs.wisc.edu/htcondor/security/vulnerabilities/CONDOR-2012-0002.html . We would recommend you update your condor to the latest version (7.8.6) released by OSG that resolves this issue.

Even though your installation is advt pbs as batch system, you seem to have local condor installation reachable through jobmanager-condor probably used by jobmanager-fork. We would recommend you upgrade this installation.

Please let us know if you have any questions.

Best
Anand
