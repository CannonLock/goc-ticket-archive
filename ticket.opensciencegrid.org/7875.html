    Jan 4, 2010 03:29 AM UTC by Ian Stokes-Rees 
    We have worked around this problem.  It was probably due to problems
with preserving escaped characters in the classad (specifically *),
however I was also having some issues with this, so I have now avoided
this problem by removing * from the argument list in my classad.

If someone else constructs a classad with something like:

Args = -X "*.zip"

then it probably won't work at FNAL.  I'll leave it up to FNAL/GOC to
decide if this constitutes a "resolved" ticket or not.  From my POV, it
is no longer a problem.

Ian
