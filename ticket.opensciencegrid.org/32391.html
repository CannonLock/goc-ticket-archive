    Hello,

I'm having trouble renewing certificates on the SE (bonner04.rice.edu), CE (bonner06.rice.edu), and Squid/GridFTP (bonner07.rice.edu) nodes of our cluster. I requested new certificates for each in November of 2016 via the "osg-cert-request" command described at [1]. I used the "-H" flag as prompted instead of "-t". I moved the old certificates to a backup folder, installed the new host certificates in "/etc/grid-security/", and moved the service certificates into "/etc/grid-security/[service name]/". As the http certificate on bonner06 expired on Nov 26th [2], the SAM tests for the CE started failing [3]. The certs were replaced, but the CE SAM tests still failed. After contacting GGUS, the OIM page entry was updated to include bonner06 as SE [4] (though I thought this had been done before). The SE worked until 12-09, when the host certificate for bonner07 expired [5]. However, this cert was replaced on November 28. During this time, the SE was not affected. However, on January 13th, the old cert for the SE expired [6] and the SE started failing. At this point I went through another round of cert replacements for the SE, CE, and Squid/GridFTP nodes. Even after this 2nd round of replacements, the tests keep failing with authentication errors.
