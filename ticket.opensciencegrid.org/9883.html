    I guess  these are "normal" in the sense that dCache/Postgresql are just responding to attempts to re-register files by logging these errors.

My "fix" is to setup postgresql.conf to have:

log_error_verbosity = terse

Then such messages consume a lot less space.  We can close this ticket.

Shawn

    Jan 30, 2011 04:15 PM UTC 
    This morning around 8 AM Eastern time our postgresql server for the dCache namespace (Chimera) filled its partition with logging info (over 10 GB in the last 24 hours).   This was traced to multiple attempts to re-register a few files over and over.

We have cleaned up space on the partition and modified the logging to be "terse" so this won't happen as easily in the future.
