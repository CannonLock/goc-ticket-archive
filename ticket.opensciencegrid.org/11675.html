> http://osggoc.blogspot.com/2011/10/goc-service-update-tuesday-october-11th.html
Added DN information to vosummary / rgsummary / scsummary / miscuser (&XMLs) pages

When the DN information was added to these pages, SQL was added to do inner join (instead of left join) with contact records(right) with DN record(left). This caused MyOSG to not display any contacts that have no associated DNs, such as mailing list, or contact for unregistered OIM users.
