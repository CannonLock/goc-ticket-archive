    Hello Bockjoo,

The WLCG topology is handled manually - the Federation Manager should inform me (lcg.office@....<mailto:lcg.office@....>) which sites in his/her federation are to be included in WLCG reporting. I then manually add the sitename to the REBUS database.

We do this manually because for whatever reason, many sites classify themselves as WLCG when in fact they are not (ie. haven’t signed an MoU with WLCG specifically, in contrast with say, an MoU with CMS or ATLAS).

There is also an issue where the mapping of WLCG terms “federation” and “site” is not the same as how resources in OSG are organised. I’m not entirely clear on the differences (Rob Quick et al can probably explain), but I do know this has caused a lot of confusion in the past and subsequently caused incorrect reporting on OSG resources.
