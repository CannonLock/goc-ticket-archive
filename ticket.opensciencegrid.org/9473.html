> It appears that glideins running on OSCER are being kicked out after 30
> minutes.  This does not occur with all of them, some seem to run fine.
>
> We are using:
> gatekeeper="grid1.oscer.ou.edu:2119/jobmanager-lsf"
> gridtype="gt2"
> rsl="(queue=hcc_q)(jobtype=single)"
That's probably because the default time limit of that queue is 30 minutes,
so you'll need to request the amount of walltime you actually need with
something like this:

rsl="(queue=hcc_q)(jobtype=single)(maxWallTime=30)

(time in minutes).

Closing this ticket again.

Cheers,

Horst
