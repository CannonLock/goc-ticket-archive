I thought I had removed this to avoid confusion, but it managed to get
packaged up due to it not being cleaned up properly in my build.  The
up-to-date OSG template is at
http://software.grid.iu.edu/pacman/tarballs/vo-version/gums.template,
which is the 1.1 format for backwards compatibility.
"gums-create-config --osg-template" will automatically download it and
make it your current configuration - it also puts in the database
password, username, and host from your existing gums.config if you
haven't specified anything.

Jay
