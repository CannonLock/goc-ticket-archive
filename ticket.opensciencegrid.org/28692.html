    Mar 1, 2016 08:45 AM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hello site admins,

In your site-local-config.xml you have section <fallback-stage-out>. The fallback-stage-out isn't required but if specified should point to an alternative site/SE. I suggest to use a neighboring site in fallback-stage-out. Otherwise this section is not useful.

List of sites that added themselves in the fallback-stage-out:
T3_MX_Cinvestav, T3_TW_NCU, T3_TW_NTU_HEP, T3_US_Baylor, T3_US_FIT, T3_US_NotreDame, T3_US_OSU, T3_US_UCD.

Example of  T3_UK_ScotGrid_ECDF site-local-config.xml:

<local-stage-out>
<command value="srmv2-lcg"/>
<option value="-S CMS_DEFAULT"/>
<catalog url="trivialcatalog_file:/exports/work/vo_cms/SITECONF/local/PhEDEx/storage.xml?protocol=srmv2"/>
<se-name value="srm.glite.ecdf.ed.ac.uk"/>
<phedex-node value="T3_UK_ScotGrid_ECDF"/>
</local-stage-out>
<fallback-stage-out>
<se-name value="gfe02.grid.hep.ph.ic.ac.uk"/>
<lfn-prefix value="srm://gfe02.grid.hep.ph.ic.ac.uk:8443/srm/managerv2?SFN=/pnfs/hep.ph.ic.ac.uk/data/cms";/>
<command value="srmv2-lcg"/>
<option value="-debug -streams_num=1"/>
<phedex-node value="T2_UK_London_IC"/>
</fallback-stage-out>
