    Hello,

As of today 06_2015.apel exists and should be automatically updated at 10 9 * * *
for the remainder of the month.

At the end of the month, things get complicated. It takes time for jobs to complete and
get into the summary data. Not to mention worker nodes with clocks off* by multiple days
and other complete randomness. We used to wait 8 days for this, I've trimmed it to three, I'm pretty
sure this will be OK but I'll check on the Monday after the 4th of July.

On July 1,2,3 the final runs of the June report are done. On these dates 06_2015.apel (with June content)
and current.epel (also with June content) will exist. On July 4-31 07_2015.apel and current.apel will exist, both
with July content.

As for older content, I have no opinion, I'd use the old format. It is quite possible you will be asked to
ressurrect the old format pages with new content. If this happens I'll let you know.

* I think it should be a felony to run a computer with an incorrect time but don't get me started
