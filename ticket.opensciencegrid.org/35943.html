    Feb 2, 2018 03:06 PM UTC by Derek Weitzel 
    Hi Ken,

Just to be clear, I want to lay out what is likely happening here.

We are getting payload records from the submit hosts such as condor:jobsub01.fnal.gov.  They do not have ProjectName set, so GRACC decides to take the VOName and place it in the ProjectName.  In many of these cases, they are things like Nova, mu2e… all of the FNAL VO’s.

Since those ProjectNames are not defined in OIM, they show up as “Unknown” in many graphs in gracc.  We would like those VO’s to have projects designated in OIM so that we can fill out things like Organization name, field of science…

A clear graph of these “Unknown” organizations is in the Site Summary page:
https://gracc.opensciencegrid.org/dashboard/db/site-summary

-Derek
