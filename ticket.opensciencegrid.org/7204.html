    Jul 15, 2009 05:43 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Hello,

On the Jul 14 2009 security team call, we discussed the issue of access control to security tickets. Currently, it's our understanding that security incidents reported by email to security@.... or by the web at <https://oim.grid.iu.edu/gocticket/security>; result in a ticket being created with Ticket Type: Security, and that tickets of this type can not be viewed by unauthenticated viewers at <https://ticket.grid.iu.edu/goc/navigator>; but can be viewed at that URL by anyone with a certificate in their browser that has been registered in OIM. I think these tickets can also be viewed by anyone with a Footprints login.

We're concerned that making security incident information available to all registered OIM users will discourage sites from providing timely and detailed incident reports to the Security Officer. Is it possible to restrict access to security incident tickets to a smaller group?

Thanks,
Jim
