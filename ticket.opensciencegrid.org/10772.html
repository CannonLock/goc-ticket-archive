From what i understand, there are a couple ways in which you can record a site as "not being available" or "inactive":

The intent of these two modules (Downtimes.py and InactiveResources.py) is to determine if there is a reason why a resource group/resource is not sending data to Gratia so that we don't raise a red flag (thereby generate a goc ticket) to resolve an issue.
