    Apr 2, 2013 02:18 PM UTC by OSG-GOC 
    Let's wait. We've got a very productive email thread with Brian, Jeff Porter,
Iwona and Suchandra going. SGE has the documented but unusual feature of
putting an extra entry into it's record file only for long running jobs (most
ATLAS jobs) exactly at midnight every night and with exit code -1. SEG is not
written to expect this and basically has to be patched to ignore the extra
records. Suchandra may be working on a patch and corresponding development rpm.

It's likely that this is the cause of the problems we see where PanDA and/or
APF thinks we have a different number of jobs than we think we have locally.
It's likely the cause of the periodic spikes of lost heartbeat jobs, local
disks filling up and the site draining due to lack of pilots when APF is
getting the wrong information from SEG via condor.
