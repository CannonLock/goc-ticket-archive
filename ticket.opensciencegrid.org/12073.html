    Hi Alain,

I'm not sure to which bit of this ticket you're referring, but if I can assume that you mean the CA chain problem, I can quote from my notes in the RT ticket from which this GOC ticket originated:

https://rt.racf.bnl.gov/rt/Ticket/Display.html?id=21880

" ...my browser (Firefox 11) still erred on the renewed certificate afterward instead of automatically importing it, due to what it reported as a CA problem [see attached].

I suspect the current instructions to download the latest CA chain are not enough: when importing the chain, users will be prompted that the import is not necessary, since the CA chain already exists and remains valid.  Instead, users will need to manually _delete_ the DOEGrids _and_ ESnet chains from their browsers and other key stores, and then re-import the new version."

In short: it has been my experience that one can't simply "renew" their ESnet and DOEGrids CA chain, and instructing users to do so may result in confusion and more tickets.

~John
