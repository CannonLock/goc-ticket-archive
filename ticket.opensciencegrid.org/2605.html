Hi,

The values that are displayed in the charts are the exact values that are
measured only when you select a very short time period (up to one day).

For larger intervals each point is the average value for the raw data in the
time interval that the point represents.

In case of one month for example, a point would be the average of ~1.5 hours
of data. For one year it would be the average of something like 18 hours. As
you select larger time intervals, you loose details.
