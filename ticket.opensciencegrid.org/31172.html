    Sep 8, 2016 02:42 PM UTC by OSG RA 
    Hi David,

After having reviewed a few more various host requests that came through yesterday, I understand your situation now.  You weren't notified
because Kevin is a GA for the same domain and VO you are a GA for in OIM.  So if a GA submits a host request, no other GAs will be
CC'd to the ticket and therefore will not be notified.  When a submitter makes a request and isn't a GA for the domain and VO, then all the GAs will
be CC'd to the ticket and are all notified of the request.  Hope this logic all makes sense to you?  Our developer review the logic on the back end
and confirmed this to be the case.

Thanks,
-Alain
