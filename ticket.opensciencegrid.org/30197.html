Hi Horst,
both the re-computations and the storage plugin causing the low A/R numbers are handled by ATLAS ADC. This  ticket is already assigned to them, but let me involve Alessandra and Bob just in case. There are ATLAS TIM meetings ongoing this week, which might have contributed to the delay in responding.

Concerning the numbers, they're based on the following measurements:
http://wlcg-sam-atlas.cern.ch/templates/ember/#/historicalsmry/heatMap?end_time=2016%2F05%2F31%2000%3A00&granularity=Daily&profile=ATLAS_CRITICAL&site=OU_OCHEP_SWT2&start_time=2016%2F05%2F01%2000%3A00&time=Enter%20Date...&view=Test%20History

taken by ETF ATLAS instance (https://etf-atlas-prod.cern.ch/etf/check_mk/index.py?start_url=%2Fetf%2Fcheck_mk%2Fdashboard.py)

As you can see there are frequent issues reported by the storage plugin on TRANSFER CHECKSUM Source and destination checksums do not match, the plugin is based on GFAL2 libraries which could be the reason why other systems don't see this (yet).

I'm not sure what could be the underlying root cause, could be an issue at the site or an issue (bug) in GFAL2 libraries, so this needs to be checked by ATLAS. In any case we don't see anything similar at any other OSG storage, so it only impacts OU.

Following the WLCG re-computation policy (https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/ArdaGrid/RequestProfileCorrections), the final reports will not be published until all recomputation requests are closed.

Cheers,
Marian
