    Hi Amy,

I did not spot the problem with the new version either, but I am not the world's foremost expert on gums config files.

As luck would have it though, when you sent the old version I was able to figure out what our problems were in the past. There is one account for the mu2e production role not present, and then in the other cases it came down to a role problem. The FQAN role is case-sensitive, and there are some groups where the account roles are "analysis" or "production" instead of "Analysis" and "Production". So to sum up I think we need the following changes for the time being:

1) New account called mu2epro. It should be identical to the existing mu2e account except that the role should be Production instead of Analysis (with a capital P.)

2) The darksideana and seaquestana accounts need the roles changed: analysis --> Analysis.

3) The darksidepro and seaquestpro accounts need the roles changed: production --> Production.

4) The "lar1" group has gone away and been replaced with the "lar1nd" group. You can just substitute "lar1nd" everywhere you see "lar1" (e.g. lar1ana --> lar1ndana). You actually don't need to change the account names themselves if you prefer not to; it should be enough to change voGroup="/fermilab/lar1" to voGroup="/fermilab/lar1nd" to get things working. I leave it to you to decide what you prefer.

Thanks very much!

Ken
