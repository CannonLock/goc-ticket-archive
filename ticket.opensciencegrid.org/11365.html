    Greetings All,
I reverted the CADist to 1.24 last night. Since there was no actual problem, this was unnecessary. But, knowing what I knew at the time and since it could do no harm, I decided to go ahead and revert and go back to bed. I'll go back to 1.25 at 10:00 (eastern) today. Expect "WARN" status after this until probes and CA updates come into synch.

I'll look into a statistically reasonable test to diagnose drops in job activity. This may not be possible because of diurnal, weekend, holiday, etc. effects but if something goes seriously wrong I'm confident our other alarm mechanisms will detect it.

As for the display issue, please see the release notice of Nov. 8th. "Please be aware the GOC makes reasonable effort to support all major browsers current versions but cannot guarantee proper behavior for all browsers and browser versions." http://osggoc.blogspot.com/2011/11/goc-service-update-tuesday-november-8th.html
