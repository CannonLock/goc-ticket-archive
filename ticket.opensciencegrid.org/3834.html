The only use of tac in the probe is in urCollector.conf, and the "write
error" is interesting because the tac command is piped into the perl:

/var/log/messages* why swap is configured but not activated on this
system, and then rectify the problem. Beyond that, I really don't know
what to do other than suggest a re-install of the GK in a more
conventional system configuration.
