    Hello Robert.

FNAL_CDFOSG_[1,2,3,4] are listed as "hidden" OSG Resources.  They must
be accessed through FNAL_FERMIGRID {fermigridosg1.fnal.gov).

See the matchmaking service guide at:

http://fermigrid.fnal.gov/matchmaking.html

For the information on how to route your job to particular internal
(hidden) FermiGrid resources.

-Keith.
