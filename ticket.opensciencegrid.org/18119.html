    Hi,

Re-running the display package locally, if I change:

VO.VOName NOT IN ('unknown', 'other')

to

VO.VOName NOT IN ('Unknown', 'unknown', 'other')

then the display and GratiaWeb agree within .1%.

I suspect we can make short work of it by doing the above for all the job queries.  We did a similar change to GratiaWeb a few months back - I suspect somewhere in the Gratia collector and/or DB, either case sensitivity matching changed or we changed 'unknown' -> 'Unknown'.
