    Jul 18, 2006 12:56 PM UTC by Tim Silvers 
    STAR uses spam protection that requires their list to be in the TO: or FROM: field or the email gets
held up. GOC notifications are sent to several addresses at once, with each address included in the
BCC: field. This hides the addresses, keeping them private. Doug O. requested the tool be modified to
send to a separate email to each address. We also need to look at what the GRNOC's tool will do for
us in this regard. Kyle, you and I will work together on this.
