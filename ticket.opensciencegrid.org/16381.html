    Aug 14, 2013 12:30 PM UTC by Soichi Hayashi 
    Marco,

I'd like to propose following in order to give you access to both VOs (OSG & UC3) as OASIS admin.

1) Create another OIM contact (with name "Marco Mambelli (cern)" and with email "marco+cern@....").
2) Move the CERN DN (/DC=ch/DC=cern/OU=Organic Units/OU=Users/CN=mambelli/CN=621693/CN=Marco Mambelli) currently associated to your existing OIM contact to the new OIM contact "Marco Mambelli (cern)"
3) Make "Marco Mambelli (cern)" the VO admin for UC3 instead of your current OIM account.

Now, this will allow oasis-login to generate gridmap file containing all of your DNs associated to OSG VO except the  CERN DN which will be associated to UC3. You can then gsissh to oasis-login using appropriate certificate by doing "gsissh ouser.uc3@...." or "gsissh ouser.osg@....".

Drawback of this approach is that, you will not be able to login to OIM using your CERN DN to your existing OIM account (you can still do so with your other DNs).

Will this approach work for you?
